【精品推荐】民国三年袁大头试铸币赏析

2020-10-29
| 游戏

1912年4月袁世凯出任大总统。北洋政府鉴于当时铸币、纸币十分复杂，流通的中外货币在百种以上，规格不一，流通混乱，折算繁琐，民众积怨，同时也想借助货币改制以解决军费问题，便决定铸发国币。袁世凯为了提高自己的统治地位，趁机把他的头像铸于币面，“袁大头”由此而来. “袁大头”首次的铸造时间是民国三年，而且该币被定为中华民国国币，因此，它在我国的钱币史上具有非常重要的地位。

Yuan shikai became President in April 1912. In view of the complexity of coinage and paper money at that time, the beiyang government decided to coin the national currency because there were more than 100 kinds of Chinese and foreign currencies in circulation, with different specifications, chaotic circulation, cumbersome conversion, and the public's resentment. In order to improve his ruling status, yuan shikai took the opportunity to cast his head on the front of the coin, which gave rise to "yuan dazhong". "yuan dazhong" was first minted in the republic of China in the third year, and the coin was designated as the national currency of the People's Republic of China, so it has a very important position in the history of Chinese COINS.

据史料记载袁大头在制造的时候是非常严谨的，不管是从重量或是成色都有一套相当严格的管理。不仅每星期要抽样送到“财务部”化验，而且中国银行也会随时派专员检查，不合格的就地熔毁，做到绝不迁就，致使每一枚留存下来的“袁大头”都是精品。而在银币收藏界袁大头还有银元之宝之称，是中国近千种银币中影响最大的银元品种。“袁大头”银元的通行促进了银元的统一，更为“废两改圆”打下了坚实的基础，极具收藏价值。

According to historical records, yuan dazhong was very strict in manufacturing, with a set of strict management from weight to finish. Not only every week to sample to the "financial department" test, and the bank of China will be sent to the commissioner at any time to check, unqualified on-site meltdown, do not compromise, so that every piece of the remaining "yuan big head" are boutique. And in the silver coin collector yuan big head is also the treasure of the silver dollar, is China nearly a thousand kinds of silver coin in the biggest impact of the silver dollar variety. The passage of "yuan big head" silver dollar promotes the unification of silver dollar, more "waste two change round" laid a solid foundation, has the collection value extremely.

中华民国所铸袁世凯像背嘉禾壹圆银币，俗称“袁大头”，在正式开铸银币前，同样有用铜冲压试机这一过程，其试机结果称为试机样币。“初始试铸币”—是指任何一款机制币，一切铸币工作已臻妥善，奉批开始铸币“后”所产出。“初始试铸币”产出后，经过检视，发现缺点，微调、改正、修图，再重新铸造，达可呈上御览核批的阶段，该批币就称为“试铸样币”。之后，还有报审“呈报样币”阶段，就是逐一向上报审批通过，之后批量生产流通的银币。而铜试机样币，非正铸品，多在冲压出来后销毁之，但是小有一些遗存下来，然其却是收藏的珍贵品种。试铸币存世量极少，流传到民间的更少。

The republic of China cast yuan shikai like back jiahe one dollar silver coin, commonly known as "yuan big head", in the official silver coin, the same process of copper stamping machine, the test results known as the test sample. "Initial trial coinage" - refers to any mechanism coinage that has been properly coinage and is produced "after" the coinage has been approved. After the "initial trial coinage" is produced, the defects are found after inspection, minor adjustment, correction, drawing modification and recasting, reaching the stage where it can be submitted to the court for approval. This batch of COINS is called "trial coinage". After that, there is the stage of "submitting sample COINS", which is to report to the examination and approval one by one, and then mass production of silver COINS in circulation. And copper test machine sample, non - casting, mostly in the stamping out after the destruction, but a few small remains, but it is a collection of precious varieties. Trial coinage exists in very small quantities, and even less is passed down to the people.

试铸币就是没有进入市场使用的。其实民国试铸币就是当时从国外买过来的机器，因为第一次铸造，用银改为铜，用铜水冷却后机器铸压，试铸币是当时的完成品样品。是后续铸银币的对照品，整个国家这种版别才几枚到十几枚而以。机制币的制作生产，从祖模设计、工具模翻制，从铜料熔炼，到压轧铜板、制作铜饼、晃洗、退火、滚边、印花等，前后涉及到的工艺流程有十几个环节，需要不同技术工种紧密配合，可以说机制币的生产过程，就是一件艺术品的绘制过程。

Trial coinage is not used in the market. In fact, the republic of China trial coinage was a machine bought from abroad at that time, because the first casting, using silver to copper, copper water cooling after the machine casting, trial coinage was the finished product samples. Is the follow-up coin, the whole country this edition only a few to a dozen. Production mechanism of currency, from ZuMo design, tools, mould making, from copper smelting, to pressure rolling copper, copper bread making, sway wash, annealing, binding, printing, etc., involved in the process before and after ten several links, need different technical work closely, can say mechanism coin production process, draw process is a work of art.

“袁大头”代表了中国近代的货币文化内涵，具有很高的观赏价值和文物价值，更有保值的功能。虽然“袁大头”在目前收藏市场上比较常见，价格也不算太高，但是由于其停止流通的时间比较久，加上解放初期时的大量兑换，造成存世量并不多，而在收藏流通中损失的也比较多，从而决定了它上升空间的庞大。另外，受价值规律的影响，这些钱币价值直线上升，并随时间推移，增值潜力越来越大。
