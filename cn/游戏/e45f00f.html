成为一个素食主义者对于冥想来说有帮助吗？

Is it helpful for meditation to become a vegetarian?

如果你一直都在吃非素食的话，成为一个素食主义者可能会改变你的生活方式。

Becoming a vegetarian could be a big lifestyle change if you have been eating non-veg your whole life.

事实上，当你成为素食主义者的时候，控制你吃的食物是支持和维护你从实践中获得的精神纯净的最简单方法。 它对于冥想来说也十分有益。

In fact, taking control of the the food you eat when you become vegetarian is one of the easiest ways to support and maintain the mental purity you gain from your practice. It's very helpful for meditation as well.

我们所吃的东西不仅影响我们的身体健康，还影响我们的思维质量和我们思想动机的质量。最终，这将影响我们的灵性健康和进步。

What we eat influences not only our physical well being, but also the quality of our mind and the quality of our thoughts and motivations. Ultimately, this affects our spiritual well being and progress.

"素食食物给我们的天性留下了深刻的烙印。如果整个世界都接纳素食主义，那它就能改变人类的命运。"--阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦

"Vegetarian food leaves a deep impression on our nature. If the whole world adopts vegetarianism,it can change the destiny of humankind."--Albert Einstein

“当我们的身体成为宰杀牲畜的活坟场，我们如何指望这个世界上有任何的和平与安康？”--乔治·萧伯纳

"While we ourselves are the living graves of murdered beasts, how can we expect any ideal conditions on this earth?"--George Bernard Shaw
