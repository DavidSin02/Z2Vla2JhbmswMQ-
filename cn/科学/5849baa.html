艺苑天下重点推介——赵小刚

Chinese artist--Xiaogang Zhao


赵小刚，字亦雄，号关中赵氏，祖籍陕西富平，中共党员，美术教师，陕西省学校艺术教育先进工作者。1958年7月出生于陕西省武功县武功镇，出身书香世家，自幼随父赵希鼎习画，先后毕业于陕西省长安师范大学，中国书画函授大学人物画专业，东方美术电视函授学院装璜系。系中国美术家协会会员，中国书法家协会会员，现居西安墨林轩。

Zhao Xiaogang, whose pen name is Yixiong. He is also called Zhao Guanzhong. His ancestral home is Fuping, Shanxi Province. He is a member of the Communist Party of China, an art teacher and an advanced worker of art education in Shanxi Province. He was born in Wugong Town, Wugong County, Shanxi Province, in July 1958. He was born in a literary family and studied painting from his father Zhao Xiding as a child. He graduated successively from Chang'an Normal University of Shanxi Province, Chinese Calligraphy and Painting Correspondence University (figure painting major), and Oriental Art and Television Correspondence College (decoration department). He is a member of Chinese Artists Association and Chinese Calligraphers Association. Now he lives in Molin Xuan, Xi'an.



《吉祥富贵图》(The tree peony)

现任中国文旅部文化艺术名誉顾问，中国文联名誉顾问，政协画院副院长，中国国画院名誉院长，世界教科文卫组织首席艺术家、艺术委员会执行委员，香港书法院国画创作室导师，台北故宫书画院名誉院长、客座教授，瑞典皇家艺术学院博士，英国皇家美术研究院教授，法兰西皇家美术学院博士，牛津艺术学院博士生导师。

He is currently an honorary consultant of the Ministry of Chinese Culture and Tourism, an honorary consultant of the Chinese Federation of Literature, the deputy president of the Painting Academy of the CPPCC, the honorary president of the Chinese Painting Academy, the chief artist and executive member of the Arts Committee of the World Esch Organization, the supervisor of the Chinese Painting Creation Room of the Hong Kong Academy of Calligraphy and Painting, the honorary president and visiting professor of the Taipei Palace Museum Painting, a doctor of Royal Swedish Academy of Art, a Professor of the Royal Academy of Fine Arts, a doctor of the Royal Academy of Fine Arts, France, and a Ph. D. supervisor of the Oxford Academy of Fine Arts.



《生生不息》(Endless life)

赵小刚曾被授予“中华当代杰出艺术家”、“国家高级美术师”、“国家特级书法师”、“新中国建设功勋人物”、“国家艺术家金奖”、“中华非遗保护者”、“国务院国宾礼特供艺术家”、“中国紫光阁国礼中心国礼艺术家”、“中华国际品牌推广中心特约书画名家”等。

He has been awarded the titles, such as "the Chinese Contemporary Outstanding Artist", "the National Senior Artist", "the National Super Calligrapher", "the Meritorious Person in the Construction of New China", "National Artist Gold Award", "Chinese Non-Heritage Protector", "the State Guest Artist under the State Council", "the State Guest Artist of Chinese Ziguang Pavilion National Ritual Center", "the Special Calligrapher and Painter of Chinese International Brand Promotion Center " and so on.



《南国风光》(Southern scenery )

其作品被联合国五大常任理事国印制成邮票全球发行;

2017年元月获联合国教科文组织“世界艺术功勋奖”;

2018年被联合国可持续发展目标推动组织评为“国际文化艺术形象大使”;

被百家媒体联合报道“一带一路2018中国当代最具卓越贡献的艺术家”。

His works have been printed by the five permanent members of the United Nations into stamps for global distribution;

In January 2017, he won "the UNESCO World Art Merit Award";

In 2018, he was named "the Ambassador of International Culture and Art Image" by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal Promotion Organization;

He was reported jointly by hundreds of media, "one of the most outstanding contribution artists in contemporary China The Belt and Road in 2018".
