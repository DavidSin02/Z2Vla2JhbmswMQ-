A two-qubit gate between phosphorus donor electrons in silicon

研究发现硅中磷给体电子之间的双量子位栅

作者：Y. He, S. K. Gorman, D. Keith, L. Kranz, J. G. Keizer , M. Y. Simmons, etc.

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1381-2

摘要：

由硅原子形成的电子自旋量子位具有较大的（数十毫伏）轨道能量和较弱的自旋轨道耦合，从而产生具有数秒相干时间的孤立电子自旋基态。

对这类量子位元的高保真度（超过99.9%）相干控制已得到证明，有望成为一个吸引人的量子计算平台。

作者使用一组完整基态上保真度约为94%的单镜头自旋读数，报告了硅中磷给体电子自旋量子位元之间的快速（约800皮秒）双量子位元交换门，通过在原子尺度上设计量子比特的位置，作者提供了一种基于硅基给体量子比特的多量子位量子电路的实现和有效表征的方法。

Abstract

Electron spin qubits formed by atoms insilicon have large (tens of millielectronvolts) orbital energies and weak spin–orbit coupling, giving rise to isolated electron spin ground states with coherence times of seconds. High-fidelity (more than 99.9 per cent) coherent control of such qubits has been demonstrated, promising an attractive platform for quantum computing. Here we report a fast (about 800 picoseconds) two-qubit exchange gate between phosphorus donor electron spin qubits in silicon using independent single-shot spin readout with a readout fidelity of about 94 per cent on a complete set of basis states. By engineering qubit placement on the atomic scale, we provide a route to the realization and efficient characterization of multi-qubit quantum circuits based on donor qubits in silicon.

Designing minimal and scalable insect-inspired multi-locomotion millirobots

研究设计出最小的多运动机器人

作者：Zhenishbek Zhakypov, Kazuaki Mori, Koh Hosoda, Jamie Paik, etc

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1388-8

摘要：

在蚁群中，集体让劳动和资源的分工具有极大的可扩展性。除了复杂的社会行为，大齿猛蚁属的蚂蚁已经发展出非凡的多运动机制，当受到威胁时，它们会突然咬住下腭，并用腿向前跳跃来越过障碍。

受这种蚂蚁启发，作者展示了一个昆虫大小的自主多运动机器人（毫米机器人），解决了小型地面机器人设计和可扩展性方面的挑战。

作者通过折叠一个由机械、材料和电子层组成的准二维超材料夹层，构建了一个手掌大小的重10克的机器人原型，这是迄今为止报道的最小、最轻的自给式多运动机器人，这将使大规模生产无装配的机器人具有高效性、灵活性和可分散性。

Abstract

In ant colonies, collectivity enables division of labour and resources with great scalability. Beyond their intricate social behaviours, individuals of the genus Odontomachus, have developed remarkable multi-locomotion mechanisms to ‘escape-jump’ upwards when threatened, using the sudden snapping of their mandibles, and to negotiate obstacles by leaping forwards using their legs. Here we demonstrate an autonomous multi-locomotion insect-scale robot (millirobot) inspired by trap-jaw ants that addresses the design and scalability challenges of small-scale terrestrial robots. We constructed the ten-gram palm-sized prototype—the smallest and lightest self-contained multi-locomotion robot reported so far—by folding a quasi-two-dimensional metamaterial sandwich formed of easily integrated mechanical, material and electronic layers, which will enable assembly-free mass-manufacturing of robots with high task efficiency, flexibility and disposability.

生物化学Biochemistry

Holistic prediction of enantios electivity in asymmetric catalysis

不对称催化中对映体选择性的整体预测

作者：Jolene P. Reid, Matthew S. Sigman

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1384-z

摘要：

当面对陌生的反应空间时，合成化学家通常会将报道的成功反应条件（试剂、催化剂、溶剂和添加剂）应用于使用新底物类型所需的密切相关反应。但由于反应要求的细微差别，这种方法往往会失败。

因此，合成化学的一个重要目标是能够定量地将化学观察结果从一个反应转移到另一个反应。作者提出了一个整体的、数据驱动的工作流，用于推导一组可用于预测样本外反应的统计模型。

这些模型揭示了传递非对称诱导的一般相互作用，并允许将这些信息定量传递到新的反应组分。该技术为将综合反应分析转化为多种化学空间、简化催化剂和反应发展创造了机会。

Abstract

When faced with unfamiliar reaction space, synthetic chemists typically apply the reported conditions (reagents, catalyst, solvent and additives) of a successful reaction to a desired, closely related reaction using a new substrate type. Unfortunately, this approach often fails owing to subtle differences in reaction requirements. Consequently, an important goal in synthetic chemistry is the ability to transfer chemical observations quantitatively from one reaction to another. Here we present a holistic, data-driven workflow for deriving statistical models of one set of reactions that can be used to predict out-of-sample reactions. These models reveal the general interactions that impart asymmetric induction and allow the quantitative transfer of this information to new reaction components. This technique creates opportunities for translating comprehensive reaction analysis to diverse chemical space, streamlining both catalyst and reaction development.

Structure and assembly of the mitochondrial membrane remodelling GTPase Mgm1

研究解析线粒体内膜结构与重塑方式

作者：Katja Faelber, Lea Dietrich, Werner Kühlbrandt & Oliver Daumke

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1372-3

摘要：

平衡融合和分裂是线粒体正常功能和生理的关键。线粒体内膜的重构是由真菌中类动力蛋白线粒体基因组维持1（Mgm1）或动物中相关蛋白视神经萎缩1 （OPA1）介导的。

作者报告了嗜热毛菌Mgm1的晶体和电子低温层析结构。Mgm1由GTPase域、束信号元件域、柄和包含膜结合位点的桨域组成。生化和细胞实验表明，Mgm1柄介导弯曲的四聚体组装成螺旋细丝。

mgm1修饰的脂质管的电子低温层析研究和重组膜管的荧光显微镜实验表明四聚体是如何在正或负弯曲膜上组装的。这一发现揭示了Mgm1和OPA1纤维是如何动态重塑线粒体内膜的。

Abstract

Balanced fusion and fission are key for the proper function and physiology of mitochondria. Remodelling of the mitochondrial inner membrane is mediated by the dynamin-like protein mitochondrial genome maintenance 1 (Mgm1) in fungi or the related protein opticatrophy 1 (OPA1) in animals. Here we present crystal and electron cryo-tomography structures of Mgm1 from Chaetomium thermophilum. Mgm1 consistsof a GTPase (G) domain, a bundle signalling element domain, a stalk, and a paddle domain that contains a membrane-binding site. Biochemical and cell-based experiments demonstrate that the Mgm1 stalk mediates the assembly of benttetramers into helical filaments. Electron cryo-tomography studies of Mgm1-decorated lipid tubes and fluorescence microscopy experiments on reconstituted membrane tubes indicate how the tetramers assemble on positively or negatively curved membranes. Our findings convey how Mgm1 and OPA1 filaments dynamically remodel the mitochondrial inner membrane.

生命科学Life Sciences

Notum produced by Paneth cells attenuates regeneration of aged intestinal epithelium

Paneth细胞产生的抑制剂削弱了肠上皮的再生

作者：Nalle Pentinmikko, Sharif Iqbal, Miyeko Mana, Simon Andersson, Pekka Katajisto, etc.

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1383-0

摘要：

在衰老过程中，干细胞功能的下降会损害组织再生，但干细胞支持生态位在衰老过程中的作用尚未得到很好的理解。小肠是由Paneth细胞生态位调控的肠干细胞主动循环来维持的。

作者研究表明由于干细胞及其生态位的缺陷，人类和小鼠肠道上皮细胞的再生潜能随着年龄的增长而减少。

这种功能下降是由于衰老的Paneth细胞中产生Notum（一种细胞外Wnt抑制剂），导致维持干细胞的Wnt信号减少。

Abstract

A decline in stem cell function impairs tissue regeneration during ageing, but the role of the stem-cell-supporting niche in ageing is not well understood. The small intestine is maintained by actively cycling intestinal stem cells that are regulated by the Paneth cell niche. Here we show that the regenerative potential of human and mouse intestinal epithelium diminishes with age owing to defects in both stem cells and their niche. The functional decline was caused by a decrease instemness-maintaining Wnt signalling due to production of Notum, an extracellular Wnt inhibitor, in aged Paneth cells.

FOXA1 mutations alter pioneering activity, differentiation and prostate cancer phenotypes

FOXA1突变改变前驱活性、分化和前列腺癌表型

作者：Elizabeth J. Adams, Wouter R. Karthaus, Christina S. Leslie, Charles L. Sawyer, etc.

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1318-9

摘要：

转录因子FOXA1的突变定义了前列腺癌的一个独特子集，但这些突变的功能后果以及它们是否会导致功能的增加或减少尚不清楚。

通过对3086例前列腺癌患者FOXA1突变的图谱进行注释，作者在叉头区定义了两个热点：Wing2（约占所有突变的50%）和高度保守的DNA接触残基R219（约占所有突变的5%）。

Wing2突变在腺癌的各个阶段都能检测到，而R219突变在具有神经内分泌组织学特征的转移性肿瘤中富集。作者通过研究野生型FOXA1和14个FOXA1突变体生物学特性，揭示了FOXA1突变体在小鼠前列腺类器官增殖实验中的功能增强。

FOXA1突变改变了前导功能，扰乱了正常的腔上皮分化程序，为谱系可塑性在癌症进展中的作用提供了进一步支持。

Abstract

Mutations in the transcription factor FOXA1 define a unique subset of prostate cancers but the functional consequences of these mutations and whether they confer gain or loss offunction is unknown. Here, by annotating the landscape of FOXA1 mutations from3,086 human prostate cancers, we define two hotspots in the forkhead domain: Wing2 (around 50% of all mutations) and the highly conserved DNA-contactresidue R219 (around 5% of all mutations). Wing2 mutations are detected in adenocarcinomas at all stages, whereas R219 mutations are enriched inmetastatic tumours with neuroendocrine histology. Interrogation of the biological properties of wild-type FOXA1 and fourteen FOXA1 mutants revealsgain of function in mouse prostate organoid proliferation assays. FOXA1 mutations alter its pioneering function and perturb normal luminal epithelial differentiation programs, providing further support for the role of lineage plasticity in cancer progression.

Comprehensivesingle-cell transcriptome lineages of a proto-vertebrate

研究解析原始脊椎动物完整单细胞转录组谱系

作者：Chen Cao, Laurence A. Lemaire, Wei Wang, Michael Levine, Kai Chen, etc.

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1385-y

摘要：

海鞘胚胎突出了细胞谱系在动物发育中的重要性。作为简单的原始脊椎动物，它们也提供了关于细胞类型进化起源的见解。

作者确定了90000多个细胞的单细胞转录组，这些细胞跨越了玻璃海鞘从开始胃泌到游泳幼虫的整个发育过程。由于海鞘胚胎中的细胞数量较少，这表示每个细胞在发育的每个阶段平均覆盖超过12倍。

作者使用单细胞转录组轨迹构建了包含幼虫神经系统的41个神经亚型的虚拟细胞谱系图和临时基因网络。他们总结了这些数据集的几个应用，包括了解游泳幼虫的突触和追踪细胞类型的进化起源。

Abstract

Ascidian embryos highlight the importance of cell lineages in animal development. As simple proto-vertebrates, they also provide insights into the evolutionary origins of cell types. Here we have determined single-cell transcriptomes for more than 90,000 cells that span the entirety of development—from the onset of gastrulation to swimming tadpoles—in Ciona intestinalis. Owing to the small numbers of cells in ascidian embryos, this represents an average of over 12-fold coverage for every cell at every stage of development. We used single-cell transcriptome trajectories to construct virtual cell-lineage maps and provisional gene networks for 41 neural subtypes that comprise the larval nervous system. We summarize several applications of these datasets, including annotating the synaptome of swimming tadpoles and tracing the evolutionary origin of cell types.

Distinct modes of mitochondrial metabolism uncouple T cell differentiation and function

线粒体代谢的不同模式使T细胞分化和功能分离

作者：Will Bailis, Justin A. Shyer, Jun Zhao, Joshua Rabinowitz, Richard A. Flavell, etc.

链接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1311-3

摘要：

在退出细胞周期并参与获得的效应因子功能之前，活化的CD4 T细胞会迅速增殖并进行表观遗传重组。在整个激活过程中，需要从原始状态进行代谢重编程。

然而，目前还不清楚这些过程是否受细胞生化成分的独立调控。作者证明了线粒体代谢的不同模式支持小鼠辅助性T细胞（TH1）的分化和效应功能，并通过生物化学解偶联这两个过程。

他们发现TH1细胞通过琥珀酸脱氢酶的末端效应作用需要三羧酸循环，琥珀酸脱氢酶活性抑制TH1细胞增殖和组蛋白乙酰化。研究数据表明，转录编程与一个平行的生化网络共同作用，以加强细胞状态。

Abstract

Activated CD4 T cells proliferate rapidly and remodel epigenetically before exiting the cell cycle and engaging acquired effector functions. Metabolic reprogramming from the naive state is required throughout these phases of activation. However, it remains unclear whether these processes are regulated independently of one another by cellular biochemical composition. Here we demonstrate that distinct modes of mitochondrial metabolism support differentiation and effector functions of mouse T helper 1(TH1) cells by biochemically uncoupling these two processes. We find that the tricarboxylic acid cycle is required for the terminal effector function of TH1 cells through succinate dehydrogenase (complex II), but that the activity of succinate dehydrogenase suppresses TH1 cell proliferation and histone acetylation. Our data suggest that transcriptional programming acts together with a parallel biochemical network to enforce cell state.
