中国情圣的成功之路

2020-10-29
| 科学

2007年, 阮琦, 网名<魔鬼咨询师>, 在豆瓣网上开了个聊天群, 专注于教男性如何与女性搭讪并成为朋友. 他在论坛中分享他的经历. 很快, 网民们蜂拥而至并开始向他咨询.

“有一种人被称为PUA(把妹达人), 他们通常衣着光鲜, 用小伎俩勾搭女性, 但这不是我,” 身着深篮色衬衣, 架着黑框眼镜, 阮以平静温和的语调告诉环球时报. 身高与相貌都并不出众的他, 在采访中显得严肃低调, 远非人们想象中的花花公子.

阮告诉环球时报, “把妹达人的目的是搭上个姑娘并最终上床. 但我所做的与他们完全不同. 我试图教会人们如何开始一段长期感情.”

爱情课程

拥有北京大学心理学学士头衔, 阮已经出版了两本教授男性如何女性交往的书, 并早在2008年已就这一专题展开授课.

根据他在豆瓣群的介绍, 花费6800元($1040), 你将获得一个课程及相关的跟进服务. 课程以一个三天实际操作开设, 接下来是三个月的网上课程, 之后是三个月的个人督导. 学员可以参加年内的活动, 参与论坛内的讨论并与他人分享自己的经验.

实际操作课程通常不超过5个学员, 内容包括对个人仪表, 肢体语言, 说话技巧的建议, 针对个人优点,弱点的分析. 课程不在课堂内教授, 而是在咖啡厅或购物中心内, 这样学员可以马上学以致用.

阮说: “我的学生大多是性格内向的男性, 年龄介于20-35岁.” 并补充到, 虽然追求异性多为男性主动, 但也有女性报名参加课程.

去年12月的一个下午, 阮带着5个学员来到北京一个购物中心,并让他们随机与女孩搭讪. 两个小时后学员回来并开始分享各自的经验. 有的学员成功拿到女孩的电话号码, 有的则还是羞于启齿与女孩交谈.

阮教开始交谈的第一步应当是 “要自然,就象是与朋友聊天, 特别注意在接近表露你真实意图时不要有任何误解.有时女生会认为你有危险, 又或者认为你只不过是一个好的销售. 这些都不是一个好的开始.”

阮的学员之一与环球时报分享他的经历. 年近30 的他,厌烦了家里不断为他安排的盲目约会,这种不满导致他跑到北京,参加了阮的一个周末培训班.

“她们不够漂亮,我想追那些颜值高的姑娘.” 就象阮的某些学员,他把女性分为高颜值和普通姑娘.

“我现在敢在街上跟高颜值姑娘打招呼了,” 他对环球时报说, “这对我来说是个进步.”

阮不仅仅只是教人们如何开始一段感情,他还提供感情咨询,诸如如何应对女友突然生气或者提出要分手的要求. 在采访中,阮不得不几次停下来接学员有关此类的紧急电话.

“我随时待命保持与学员的联系.”

广阔应用前景

虽然他可能是先行者,但阮并不是唯一提供这种课程的人. 实际上,阮觉得这种课程现在已经形成一种产业了. 有的PUA有自己的公司和雇员,有的在电视上炫耀他们的把妹技巧,比如如何在一分钟内从随机挑选的姑娘手中拿到手机号码.

市场对PUA的需求是巨大的. 在中国大陆,接近2亿的成年人是单身.2013年,在中国约有14.6%的人没有另一半,而这一数字在2006年仅仅是6%. 据羊城晚报报道,有些专家预测,到2020年,中国大陆的单身男性将达3至3千5百万,一项婚姻调查报告显示,19%的被访者表示,之所以单身是由于缺乏与异性沟通的技能.

不管怎样,有人质疑爱可以教吗？

阮说: “我所做的只是教人们沟通技巧及如何应对问题.”并提到有些公司请他去给员工授课,以便员工学会如何与女顾客交流.

“建立自然,友善的联系是某种可以在多个领域应用的东西.”

China’s answer to ‘Hitch’ reveals the key to his success

By Xiong Yuqing Source:Global Times Published: 2016-2-13 18:33:01



In 2007, Ruan Qi, known as Mogui Zixunshi (lit. evil consultant) online, started a group on douban.com that was dedicated to teaching men how to start conversations with women and become friends with them. He shared his personal experiences in this forum, which soon became very popular among netizens who flooded in to ask for his advice.

"There are people called PUA (pick-up artists), who often wear exaggerated clothing and try to pick up girls with tricks. That's not who I am," Ruan told the Global times in a gentle but calm tone. Of average height and looks and wearing a dark navy shirt and black-framed glasses, Ruan came across as low-key and serious during our interview, a far cry from most people's idea of a playboy.

"A PUA's goal is to date a girl and finally get her in bed. But what I'm doing is very different from that. I'm trying to teach people how to start a long-term relationship," Ruan told the Global Times.

A course in love

Graduated from Peking University with a BA in Psychology, Ruan has published two books that teach men how to pick-up women. He also began holding courses on the subject starting back in 2008.

According to the introduction to his Douban group, 6,800 yuan ($1,040) gets you the course and follow-up service. The course starts with a three-day practical course, then a three-month online course and then three months of personal supervision. Student members can attend events throughout the year and can join discussion forums where they can share their experience.

A class for the practical course usually consists of five students or less and covers advice on personal appearance, body language, language usage and an analysis of each individual's strengths and weaknesses. Courses are not held in classrooms, but in cafes at downtown shopping malls so students can put into practice what they learn as soon as they can.

"Most of my students are introverted, male, and between the ages of 20 to 35," Ruan said, adding that while chasing the opposite sex is more often left to men sometimes even women will attend a class.

One afternoon last December, Ruan took five male students to a shopping mall in Beijing where he had them randomly pick up girls. After two hours, they returned to Ruan and shared their results. Some of them had successfully gotten some women's numbers, while some still lacked the courage to even start a conversation.

Ruan teaches that the first step in starting a conversation is that "it should just be natural, just like talking between friends, and make sure you avoid any misunderstanding when it comes to your real intentions. Sometimes girls might think that you're dangerous, or see you as being a good salesman. That's not a good start."

One of Ruan's students shared his experience with the Global Times. Coming up on 30, he was not satisfied with the blind dates his family was constantly setting him up on. This dissatisfaction encouraged him to head all the way to Beijing to attend one of Ruan's weekend classes.

"They were not pretty enough. I want to chase those 'high-score girls.'" Like some of Ruan's other students, he divides women into "high-score girls" and everyone else.

"I now have the courage to say hello to a 'high-score girl' on the street. That's an improvement for me," he told the Global Times.

Ruan doesn't just teach how to start off a relationship, he also provides relationship advice such as how to deal with emergencies such as when a woman becomes suddenly angry or decides to break up with her boyfriend. During our interview, Ruan had to pause several times to answer students dealing with such emergencies.

"I keep in touch with my students and stand by them all the time," he said.

Broader application

Although he may be one of the earliest, Ruan is not the only teacher offering such classes. In fact, Ruan feels that these classes have grown to become an industry now. Some PUAs have their own companies staffed with employees. Some show off their pick-up skills on TV shows, like how they can get a telephone number from a random girl in under a minute.

Market demand for PUAs is huge. There are nearly 200 million single adults in the Chinese mainland. Around 14.6 percent of people in China were not in a relationship in 2013, while this number was only 6 percent in 1990. According to Yangcheng Evening News, some experts predict that there will be about 30-35 million single men in the Chinese mainland by 2020, while a survey on marriage revealed that 19 percent of respondents said that they were single because they lacked the ability to communicate with the opposite sex.

However, some wonder if love is something that can be taught.

"What I'm doing is teaching people the skills they need to communicate and deal with problems," Ruan said, mentioning that some enterprises have also invited him to give courses to employees so these employees can use techniques used to talk with women on customers.

"Creating a friendly and natural connection is something that can be applied in many different fields."
