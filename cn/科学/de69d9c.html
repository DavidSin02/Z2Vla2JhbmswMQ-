国家艺术家金奖、赵小刚博士艺术简介：赵小刚、男、汉族、祖籍陕西富平、1958年7月出生于陕西省武功县武功镇。出身书香门弟、博士学位、美术教师、书画家、现居西安墨林轩。获陕西省学校艺术教育先进工作者、国家艺术家金奖、新中国建设功勋人物、国·务·院·国宾礼特供艺术家、中·华·人·民·共·和·国·文化和旅游部文化艺术名誉顾问。国家高级美术师、国家特级书法师、中国书法家协会会员、中国美术家协会会员、中华非遗保护者、全国政协画院执行副院长、中国国画院名誉院长、世界华人总会陕西书画研究院院长、台北故宫书画院名誉院长客座教授、香·港·书法院国画创作室导师、世界教科文卫组织首席艺术家艺术委员会持行委员、瑞典皇家艺术学院博士、英国皇家美术研究院教授、法兰西皇家美术学院文学艺术博士、牛津艺术学院博士生导师、其作品被·联·合·国·常任理事国印制成邮票册全球发行、2017年元月获联合国教科文组织“世界艺术功勋奖”、2018年被联合国可持续发展推动组织评选为“国际文化艺术形象大使”、并被百家媒体联合报道“一·带·一·路·2018中国当代最具卓越贡献的艺术家”、2019年7月获“中国最具影响力50位书画名家荣誉称号”等。作品润格：书法作品2万元/平尺、国画作品6万元/平尺。

National Artist Gold Award and Dr. Zhaoxiaogang's Art Introduction: Zhaoxiaogang, male, Han nationality, native of Shaanxi Fuping, was born in Wugong Town, Wugong County, Shaanxi Province in July 1958. Born in Shu Xiangmendi, Ph.D., art teacher, painter, and now lives in Xianmolinxuan. He was awarded the Shaanxi Provincial School Art Education Advanced Worker, the National Artist Gold Award, the New China Construction Meritorious Person, the State Council State Binli Special Artist, and the Honorary Consultant of the Ministry of Culture and Tourism of the People's Republic of China. National Senior Artist, National Special Calligraphers, Member of the Chinese Calligraphers Association, Member of the Chinese Artists Association, Chinese Heritage Protector, Executive Vice President of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Painting Academy, Honorary President of the Chinese Painting Academy, Dean of the Shaanxi Painting and Painting Institute of the World Chinese Association, Honorary President of the Taipei School of Calligraphy and Painting, Mentor of the Chinese Painting Studio of the Hong Kong Calligraphy Court, World UNESCO The organization's chief artist art committee member, doctor of the Royal Swedish Academy of Arts, professor of the Royal British Academy of Fine Arts, doctor of literature and art of the Royal French Academy of Fine Arts, doctoral tutor of the Oxford Academy of Arts, and his works were printed as a stamp album by the permanent members of the United Nations. Published, won the UNESCO "World Art Merit Award" in January 2017, and was awarded in 2018 The United Nations Sustainable Development Promotion Organization was selected as the "International Cultural and Art Image Ambassador" and was jointly reported by 100 media as "The Belt and Road 2018 Chinese Contemporary Artists with Outstanding Contributions".
