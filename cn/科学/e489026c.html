"Congratulations" is a song we are familiar with. It is a song that we have listened to and sung many times. However, this song was not originally used to celebrate the New Year. In modern China, during that war era, people were not only clothed in clothes. Can't eat enough, and escapes in the rain of bullets every day. Until the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan in 1945, people began to have the courage to look forward to tomorrow. In order to celebrate the first New Year after the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan, Chen Gexin composed the song "Congratulations" under the pen name of "Qing Yu", under the pen name of "Qing Yu". "Yu" is because I was fortunate to survive the War of Resistance. The original song "Congratulations" does not have the bright feeling of the Chinese New Year's gongs and drums. It is just the sadness of the guitar chord accompaniment.

The lyrics "winter" , "Spring Breeze", and "Plum Blossom" all refer to the cold winter ice and snow for the life and death of the Chinese nation. The arrival of victory is the blossoming of spring flowers. At that time, congratulations congratulations are not congratulations on a happy new year but congratulations on being alive. Later, the song was sung by many people, and many other elements were added. The song seemed relaxed and lively and became joyful.

After understanding the creative background of this song, you can understand the reason why each sentence of the lyrics is written in this way. The victory of the war and the ushering of new hope are the rest of the life. We must remember history and move forward.
