  • 词汇表达

2. IDK=I don't know

3. FYI=For your information

4. LOL=Laugh out loud

  • 拓展训练

1. BTW, thinking back, I didn't buy my father any present.

我也不知道，也许你应该再问问她。


3. FYI, don't miss out the greatest opportunity!

知会一声，别错过一个绝佳的机会。


4. It was pretty dangerous by bus today,

LOL!今天搭公车可真够危险的，哈哈！
