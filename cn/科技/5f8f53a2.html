Brightening detergent ZD-585

Application characteristics

The brightening detergent ZD-585 can remedy the insufficient oxidation and sub-chromogenicity of indigo dyeing. After the back washing, the surface color is basically unchanged in depth, and the color of yarn skip to brilliant blue. Moreover, the purity and fastness were increased, and its floating color was reduced. Water sample results showed a marked decrease in COD. After sizing, the cloth was finished. And then put it in the open air for a period of time, and the color changing situation of the cloth was also improved significantly.
