HOLIDAY BRYNLEIGH (CONT'D)

(still suspicious) (over) If my mom died when she was -- not your mom-- 11...I mean if what you are saying is true, then why wouldn’t Cyrus tell me? We have a--

那么他为什么不 -

因为我告诉他不要！

Because, I saved him in the swamp. Well, we saved each other. And because I thought maybe, for once, we could all have a nice Christmas. Even you. He wanted to tell you. But you had your snazzy new hoverboard... You found your grandpa and you were...happy.

因为，我救了他在沼泽地。好吧，我们互相拯救了。而且因为我想也许，一次，我们都可以度过一个美好的圣诞节。即便是你。他想告诉你。但是你有一个时髦的新悬浮板......你找到了你的爷爷，你很高兴。

BRYNLEIGH (CONT'D)

好吧。但如果那是真的，爷爷 -

--Not Grandpa--

- 不是爷爷 -

随你！如果那个让我们吃奶昔并送给我们圣诞礼物的人......如果Whittier博士带走了Cyrus，那么为什么Angelica Graves同意见到我的妈妈呢？

BRYNLEIGH

(shocked) I’m sorry, what now?

对不起，现在怎么办？

HOLIDAY

They’re down at Jackson Square right now, trying to get Cyrus back from her!

他们现在正在杰克逊广场，试图让赛勒斯从她身边回来！

BRYNLEIGH

什么？！他们联系了她？！

HOLIDAY

I mean, yeah, Mom called her and--

我的意思是，是的，妈妈叫她 -

It’s a set up. The whole thing is a trap!

这是一个陷阱。整个事情是一个陷阱！



It’s crowded, loud. Groups of tourists delight in the tastes and sights.

MONICA

I’ll head over to St. Louis Cathedral to wait for Angelica. You find a hiding place nearby. Let me know if you spot anyone else lurking.

我将前往圣路易斯大教堂等待当归。你在附近找到一个藏身之处。如果你发现潜伏的其他人，请告诉我。

JAMES

Monica -

莫妮卡 -

Yes, you think this is a terrible idea. I have no idea what Angelica will have in store for me. I get it.

是的，你认为这是一个糟糕的主意。我不知道Angelica会为我准备什么。我知道了。

JAMES

I was going to say I’m proud of you.

我会说我为你感到骄傲。

MONICA

Yeah?

是吗？

Always. I’m a lucky man. My wife is one bad woman.

总是。我是一个幸运的人。我的妻子是一个坏女人。

噢，这是你曾经对我说的最好的事情。

JAMES

One other thing...

另一件事......

MONICA

I love you, too, James.

詹姆斯，我也爱你。

JAMES

(caught off guard) Oh, I...I love you, too, Monica. But I was going to say...I have a plan.

（猝不及防）哦，我......我也爱你，莫妮卡。但我会说...我有一个计划。



CLANGING and SCRAPING as Cyrus crawls through the small air vent, dragging Hermione with him.

当Cyrus爬过小通风口时，抓住Cl and，将Hermione拖到他身边。

CYRUS

(with the grunts of exertion)

（随着努力的咕噜声）

(MORE)

Funny how stuffy an air vent is, huh, Hermione. (grunt) Point your light up a bit more, please... The sound of Hermione repositioning her light.

有趣的是，通风口是多么闷，呵呵，赫敏。 （咕噜声）请点亮你的灯，拜托......赫敏重新定位她的光芒的声音。

CYRUS (CONT’D)

(grunt) Thanks! That’s better.

（咕噜）谢谢！那更好。

HERMIONE

I have another funny joke, Cyrus. Why was the robot angry?

我有另一个有趣的笑话，赛勒斯。机器人为什么生气？

CYRUS

Beats me.

不知道。

HERMIONE

Because someone kept pushing her buttons.

因为有人不停地按下按钮。

CYRUS

Good one. (grunt) Is it just me or is it feeling a little claustrophobic in here? (grunt) At least we’re going up...I think. (deep breaths, starting to panic) Stay calm Cyrus. Just keep going. You can do this. Hear a LOW BATTERY ALARM

好的。 （咕噜）这只是我还是在这里感觉有点幽闭？ （咕噜声）至少我们要上升......我想。 （深呼吸，开始恐慌）保持冷静赛勒斯。尽管继续。你可以这样做。听到低电量警报

HERMIONE

Battery at 20%. Please recharge.

电池电量为20％。请充电。

CYRUS

Oh, no, no, no. Don’t die now. You’re the only company I have.

哦，不，不，不。不要死了。你是我唯一的伙伴。

HERMIONE

Please recharge.

请充电。

CYRUS

Why didn’t I implement a low power mode when we built you? Oh, right, because I didn’t anticipate having to drag you through an air vent to save us from being buried alive.

我们建造你时为什么不实施低功耗模式？哦，是的，因为我没想到要把你拖过通风口以免我们被活埋。

(MORE)

CYRUS (CONT'D)

Note to self: when building future AI, consider all possible scenarios.

自我注意：在构建未来的AI时，请考虑所有可能的情况。

HERMIONE

Please recharge.

请充电。

Ughhh。赫敏，请不要放弃我。你真的照亮了道路。如果我输了 - 我们听到赛勒斯嘎嘎作响。

CYRUS (CONT’D)

Hey. It’s a grate! It looks like it opens down into a room. Maybe there’s an outlet to plug you in. I just need to...Kick. (grunt as he kicks the grate) The Grate. (grunt, kicking again) In! The grate falls to the ground.

嘿。这是一个排风口！看起来它打开了一个房间。也许有一个插座可以插入你。我只需要......踢。 （咕噜咕噜地踢着排风口）。 （咕噜，再次踢）In！排风口倒在地上。

CYRUS (CONT’D)

Yes!! Okay, Hermione. I’ll jump down. Then I’ll catch you.

是！！好的，赫敏。我会跳下去的。然后我会抓住你的。

HERMIONE

Knock knock.

敲敲。

CYRUS

Not now, Hermione.

不是现在，赫敏。

HERMIONE

敲敲。

是的，我知道。好的。在grouuuuuuund上见到你......当赛勒斯从天花板上面跳进房间时，

爸爸刚发。他们在杰克逊广场的会合点附近。

Well, tell them to get out of there before Angelica sees them. Abandon ship!

好吧，告诉他们在Angelica看到他们之前离开那里。弃船！

HOLIDAY

但如果她确实有赛勒斯 -

She doesn’t!

她没有！

HOLIDAY

你肯定不知道！

Freakshow, did you hear anything I said before? WHY would I be lying about this?

Holiday，你有没有听过我之前说过的话？我为什么要对此撒谎？

Because you’re YOU.

因为你就是你。

(takes a breath) Okay. I say mean things, I hurt people’s feelings for sport...but I don’t lie. If Cyrus were here -

（深吸一口气）好的。我说的是卑鄙的事情，我伤害了人们对运动的感觉......但我不撒谎。如果赛勒斯在这里 -

他不是。她不见了。

BRYNLEIGH

Where are you going?!

你要去哪里？！

This conversation is pointless. I’m going to find my brother!

这次谈话毫无意义。我要去找我的兄弟！

但如果他是这样的话 - 我应该和Birdie和Grampa Cray-Cray一起做什么呢？门砰的一声。

哦，好，你的意识到了 -

小鸟 - 我以为你是Holiday。

谢谢！但不是。 （击败）哦哦。现在怎么了？

BIRDIE

（叹气）让我猜一下。他是坏人，对吧？

BRYNLEIGH

What? How did you know?!

什么？你怎么知道？！

BIRDIE

你见过我的生活吗？他们都变成了坏人。

BRYNLEIGH

他们？除了你的妈妈和爸爸。还有赛勒斯。

BRYNLEIGH

What’s next?

下一步是什么？

BIRDIE

(re-energized) What’s our next move? Let’s get on this!

（重新充满活力）我们的下一步行动是什么？让我们来吧！

BRYNLEIGH

(laughs) I always liked you, Bird.

（笑）我一直很喜欢你，伯德。

BIRDIE

You called me Bratty Anders.

你叫我Bratty Anders。

BRYNLEIGH

A few times.

几次。

BIRDIE
