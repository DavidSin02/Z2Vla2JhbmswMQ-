中国台州当代美术馆近日完工

taizhou contemporary art museum by atelier deshaus nears completion in china

大舍建筑设计事务所完成了对浙江省一混凝土厂房的结构改造，室内装修也一并竣工。室内工程完工之后，“台州当代美术馆”将成为当地的文化地标，同时与其他改造后的工厂及仓库一同对外开放。因此，这座美术馆是该项目的核心建筑，并且与周边环境形成良好的对话。

Shanghai-based firm atelier deshaus has completed the structural renovation of a concrete industrial building in china’s zhejiang province. once the interiors are also complete, the ‘taizhou contemporary art museum’ will open as part of a new cultural destination, joining other converted factories and warehouse buildings. consequently, as the project’s centerpiece, the art museum intends to initiate a dialogue with its neighbors.

all images by tian fangfang, courtesy of atelier deshaus

台州当代美术馆总建筑面积2450平方米，内设8个展厅。建筑内部形成丰富的空间序列，相邻楼层错动排列，不会形成视觉上的陡峭冲击。为表现建筑的真实状态，建筑师委托艺术家尹毅记录下其原始外观形态，并将视频放置于项目简介之中。

comprising eight exhibition rooms, the ‘taizhou contemporary art museum’ occupies a total construction area of 2,450 square meters (26,371 sqf). internally, a rich spatial sequence has been established with adjacent floors staggered to alleviate any sense of steepness. to illustrate the building’s current state, atelier deshaus commissioned chinese artist yin yi to document its raw appearance as part of a new video (see below).

项目位于中国浙江省／the industrial building is located in china’s zhejiang province

建筑师通过现浇混凝土和桶状拱形结构形成的空间对比，进一步强化了空间的独特氛围。游客将通过毗邻室外广场的入口大堂进入建筑内部，包含有咖啡厅和艺术品商店的入口大堂在需要时还将作为举办公共活动的场所。游客将沿着旋转通道来到顶楼的主展厅，在这里可以清晰地看见附近延绵的山脉。

the distinct spatial atmosphere is further amplified through the contrast of the cast in-situ concrete and the spaces formed by the barrel vault structure. visitors will enter the building via a lobby, which adjoins an external public square. the lobby, which has been designed with the potential of hosting public events, will also include a café and art store. guests will ascend the building in a spiraling motion, before reaching the main exhibition room on the top floor. from here there will be a clear view of the nearby mountain range.
