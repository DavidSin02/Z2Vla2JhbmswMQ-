工作原理

AF系列振动筛选机的工作原理是利用振动电机上产生的离心力得到激振动力来实现的，激振动力使筛体及物料进行反复 上下运动，再经过两层筛网的筛选，有效的将物料按照筛网的孔径范围分为三种粗细等级，从而达到理想的筛分效果

Working Principle

The working principle of the AF series vibration screening machine is realized by using the centrifugal force generated on the vibration motor to obtain the exciting vibration force , The vibration force causes the sieve body and the material to repeatedly move up and down , and then through the screening of the two-layer screen , the material is effectively divided into three thickness grades according to the pore size range of the screen , thereby achieving the desired screening effect


脱水风干振动筛

工作原理

本机主要针对塑料行业水环模头切，水下切之后的塑料粒子中含水的处理，因传统的离心脱水机噪音大，易堵网且容易将颗粒打碎，故障率高等原因，用户急需一种设备可以取代，恩必信独立研发的这款震动脱水风干机应动而生，经客户实际试用评测，本设备处理过后的颗粒可达到脱水效果，且一机多用能耗降低30%

Dehydrated Air Dried Vibrating Screen

Working Principle

This machine is mainly used for the water ring die cutting in the plastic industry , and the water treatment is the plastic particles after underwater cutting . Because the traditional centrifugal dewatering machine has high noise , easy to block the net and easily break the particles , and the failure rate is high , the user urgently needs a device to replace . The vibration dehydration air dryer independently developed by Enbike came into being . After the actual trial evaluation by the customer , the particles after the treatment of the equipment can achieve the dehydration effect , and the energy consumption of one machine is reduced by 30%

特点

所有与原料接触部分全为SUS304不锈钢材质

震动脱水、加热风干燥可分开控制

自带水料分离装置和水箱，可以实现脱水后的水循环利用

物料整个风干过程都是处理一个无损状态，不会产生粉屑

Features

All parts in contact with raw materials are all SUS304 stainless steel

Vibration dehydration , heating air drying can be controlled separately

Self-contained water separation device and water tank for water recycling after dehydration

The whole air drying process of the material is in a non-destructive state , and no dust is generated
