产品简介PRODUCT OVERVIEW

PC12系列开关是用于工业过程中的普及型开关，它采用一体式结构，外壳连 线方便，坚固耐用。本产品结构紧凑，使用简便，有良好的经济性，可广泛 地应用于各种控制过程中。 PC 12 Series switch is a low cost switch in industrial process, features one-piece enclosure, easy wiring, durable performance. This product is widely used in different control processes for its compact design, simple handling and economic purpose.
