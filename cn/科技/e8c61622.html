Elbow refers to a kind of pipe fittings used at the bend of pipeline. Because the water pipe itself is straight and can not bend, if you want to change the direction of the water pipe, you can only achieve it through the elbow, mainly 45 degree elbow and 90 degree elbow. Sometimes, the outer and inner elbows are also used. The outer elbow is also known as the Yang elbow and the outer thread elbow. The inner elbow is also known as the Yinkou elbow and the inner thread elbow. Internal and external wires are used when connecting faucets, water meters and other types of water pipes. They are usually used together. Internal wires are the main components used in household decoration. Ribbon joint fittings are more expensive, and sometimes a ribbon joint is more expensive than a meter water pipe.
