溫柔文案丨一想到你，就覺得人間可愛

2020-11-14
| 科學

你要做個可愛的姑娘，不煩世事，滿心歡喜。

You want to be a lovely girl, not bothered by the world, full of joy.


我的小熊說它真的很想見你，對了，我也是。

My little bear says he really wants to see you. By the way, so do I.


有時候挺討厭自己的，不會說話，太過善良，沒什麼心眼還這麼可愛。

Sometimes I hate myself very much. I don't speak smoothly. I'm too kind. I don't have any idea. I'm so cute.


這個世界上，從來就沒有最好的，只有最合適的。比如雲朵和天空，微風和草地，比如我眼中的你，以及，你眼中的我。

In this world, there is never the best, only the most suitable. Like clouds and sky, breeze and grass, like you in my eyes, and I in your eyes.


大概是好久沒有認識新朋友，今天突然被一句禮貌的話擊中了：很高興認識你。

我也很高興，就好像幼兒園時代，我們在鞦韆旁分享了最後一塊兒牛奶餅乾，你對我伸出手說：“今天和你玩得特別開心，我們明天也一起玩好不好。 ”

I haven't known a new friend for a long time. Today, I was hit by a polite saying: "nice to meet you."

I'm also very happy. It's like kindergarten. We shared the last milk biscuit beside the swing. You extended your hand to me and said, "have a special time with you today, and we'll have a good time tomorrow."


我希望你做一個甜甜的夢，我的意思是，夢到我。

I hope you have a sweet dream. I mean, dream about me.


就像迪斯尼樂園，所有的小孩子即使摔倒了也不會哭，為什麼呢？因為玩都來不及，要趕快爬起來去玩。如果你把自己的人生當作遊樂場，你跌倒了就不會哭。

Like Disneyland, all children can't cry even if they fall down. Why? Because it's too late to play, we need to get up and play. If you regard your life as a playground, you will not cry when you fall down.


一想到你，就覺得人間可愛。

When I think of you, I think the world is lovely.

￼

我是個在過六一的孩子，為什麼我要面對這麼多。

I'm a kid on children's day. Why should I face so much.
