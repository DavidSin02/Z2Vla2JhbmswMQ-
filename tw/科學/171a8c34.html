One thousand plant transcriptomes and the phylogenomics of green plants

1000種植物轉錄組和綠色植物的系統基因組學

作者：James H. Leebens-Mack, Michael S. Barker, Eric J. Carpenter, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1693-2

摘要：

綠色植物包括約45萬~50萬個豐富多樣的物種，並且在陸地和水生生態系統中具有重要作用。

這裡，作為“1000種植物轉錄組計劃”的一部分，我們對1124種廣義植物的營養轉錄組進行了測序。這些植物包括綠色植物、藍藻和紅藻。我們的分析為研究綠色植物的進化提供了一個強有力的系統基因組框架。

值得注意的是，我們發現在綠色植物、陸生植物和維管植物起源之前，基因家族就已經大量擴展，而整個基因組的複製在開花植物和蕨類植物的進化過程中反覆發生。

高質量植物基因組序列的不斷增加和功能基因組學的進步，使基因組進化的研究跨越生命之樹。

Abstract

Green plants (Viridiplantae) include around 450,000–500,000 species of great diversity and have important roles in terrestrial and aquatic ecosystems. Here, as part of the One Thousand Plant Transcriptomes Initiative, we sequenced the vegetative transcriptomes of 1,124 species that span the diversity of plants in a broad sense (Archaeplastida), including green plants (Viridiplantae), glaucophytes (Glaucophyta) and red algae (Rhodophyta). Our analysis provides a robust phylogenomic framework for examining the evolution of green plants. Notably, we find that large expansions of gene families preceded the origins of greenplants, land plants and vascular plants, whereas whole-genome duplications are inferred to have occurred repeatedly throughout the evolution of flowering plants and ferns. The increasing availability of high-quality plant genomesequences and advances in functional genomics are enabling research on genome evolution across the green tree of life.

Arthropod decline in grasslands and forests is associated with landscape-level drivers

草原和森林節肢動物的減少與景觀水平的驅動因素有關

作者：Sebastian Seibold, Martin M. Gossner, Nadja K. Simons, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1684-3

摘要：

最近關於節肢動物物種局部滅絕和節肢動物生物量大量下降的報道指出，土地利用集約化是生物多樣性減少的主要原因。

這裡，我們分析了100多萬個節肢動物個體（約2700個物種）的數據。這些數據來自德國3個地區的150個草地和140個森林點、採集自2008~2017年的標準化存儲庫。

隨著時間的推移，草原和森林的總體伽瑪多樣性下降，表明了不同地點和地區的物種消失。

在每年取樣的草地上，生物量、丰度和物種數量分別下降了67%、78%和34%。營養水平的下降是一致的，主要影響稀有物種；其規模與當地的土地利用強度無關。然而，在農業用地覆蓋率較高的景觀中嵌入的地點顯示出較強的時間衰退。

Abstract

Recent reports of local extinctions of arthropod species, and of massive declines in arthropod biomass, point to land-use intensification as a major driver of decreasing biodiversity. Here we analyse data from more than 1 million individual arthropods (about 2,700 species), from standardized inventories taken between 2008 and 2017 at 150 grassland and 140 forest sites in 3 regions of Germany. Overall gamma diversity in grasslands and forests decreased over time, indicating loss of species across sites and regions. In annually sampled grasslands, biomass, abundance and number of species declined by 67%, 78% and34%, respectively. The decline was consistent across trophic levels and mainly affected rare species; its magnitude was independent of local land-use intensity. However, sites embedded in landscapes with a higher cover of agricultural land showed a stronger temporal decline.

材料科學Material Science

Polymers with controlled assembly and rigidity made with click-functional peptide bundles

“點擊”功能性肽束製成的擁有受控裝配和剛性的聚合物

作者：Dongdong Wu, Nairiti Sinha, Jeeyoung Lee, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1683-4

摘要：

生物分子改造是高功能、複雜軟材料設計中的一個關鍵概念。

生物分子具有廣泛的功能和結構，包括化學識別（如對酶底物或黏附配體的識別）、精細的納米結構（由肽、蛋白質或核酸組成）和不尋常的機械性能（如絲樣強度、剛度、粘彈性和彈性）。

這裡，我們將物理（非共價）相互作用的計算設計與路徑相關的、分級的“點擊”共價組裝相結合，以產生混合合成肽基聚合物。

這些聚合物納米尺度的單體單元是很多捆均質四聚體的、α-螺旋狀低分子量肽的總和。這些捆綁單體，或“捆綁物”，可以被設計成對化學功能的穩定性、大小和空間展示提供完全控制。

Abstract

The engineeringof biological molecules is a key concept in the design of highly functional, sophisticated soft materials. Biomolecules exhibit a wide range of functionsand structures, including chemical recognition (of enzyme substrates or adhesive ligands, for instance), exquisite nanostructures (composed of peptides,proteins or nucleic acids), and unusual mechanical properties (such assilk-like strength, stiffness, viscoelasticity and resiliency). Here we combine the computational design of physical (noncovalent) interactions with pathway-dependent, hierarchical ‘click’ covalent assembly to produce hybridsynthetic peptide-based polymers. The nanometre-scale monomeric units of thesepolymers are homotetrameric, α-helical bundles of low-molecular-weight peptides. These bundled monomers, or ‘bundlemers’, can be designed to provide complete control of the stability, size and spatial display of chemical functionalities.

Superconductors, orbital magnets and correlated states in magic-angle bilayer grapheme

石墨烯中的超導體、軌道磁體和相關態

作者：Xiaobo Lu, Petr Stepanov, Wei Yang, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1695-0

摘要：

超導電性可在接近破缺對稱性母態的條件下發生。在雙層石墨烯中，一層相對於另一層以大約1度的“神奇”扭轉角扭曲，導致了超平坦莫爾超晶格微帶的出現。這樣的波段是強相關物理的一個豐富和高度可調的來源，尤其是出現在接近交互誘導的絕緣狀態的超導性。

這裡，我們報道了具有高度均勻扭角的磁角扭轉雙層石墨烯器件的製備。

扭轉角障礙的減少揭示了絕緣狀態在所有整數佔用的4倍自旋谷簡併平傳導和價帶中的存在，即在莫爾波段填充因子ν=0、±1、±2、±3時。在ν≈−2時,超導電性在臨界溫度達3開爾文以下時被觀測到。

Abstract

Superconductivity can occur under conditions approaching broken-symmetry parent states. In bilayer graphene, the twisting of one layer with respect to the other at‘magic’ twist angles of around 1 degree leads to the emergence of ultra-flat moiré superlattice minibands. Such bands are a rich and highly tunable source of strong-correlation physics, notably superconductivity, which emerges closeto interaction-induced insulating states. Here we report the fabrication of magic-angle twisted bilayer graphene devices with highly uniform twist angles.The reduction in twist-angle disorder reveals the presence of insulating statesat all integer occupancies of the fourfold spin–valley degenerate flat conduction and valence bands—that is, at moiré band filling factors ν = 0, ±1,±2, ±3. At ν ≈ −2, superconductivity is observed below critical temperatures of up to 3 kelvin.

氣候學/天文學

Climatology/Astronomy

Two-million-year-old snapshots of atmospheric gases from Antarctic ice

來自南極冰川的大氣氣體的200萬年前“快照”

作者：Yuzhen Yan, Michael L. Bender, Edward J. Brook, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1692-3

摘要：

過去80萬年間，冰期—間冰期旋迴以10萬年的週期（“100k世界”）振盪。約280萬~120萬年前，冰川旋迴的規模較小且持續時間較短（“40k世界”）。

來自深海沉積物的數據表明，40k世界的大氣二氧化碳的變化幅度也低於100k世界，但我們並未直接觀測到這一時期的大氣溫室氣體。

最新研究結果證實，在40k世界，大氣溫室氣體和南極氣候的冰期—間冰期變化幅度減小，同時從40k世界向100k世界的轉變伴隨著冰川達到最大值期間最低二氧化碳濃度的下降。

Abstract

Over the past eight hundred thousand years, glacial–interglacial cycles oscillated with aperiod of one hundred thousand years (‘100k world’). Between about 2.8 and 1.2million years ago, glacial cycles were smaller in magnitude and shorter induration (‘40k world’). Proxy data from deep-sea sediments suggest that the variability of atmospheric carbon dioxide in the 40k world was also lower than in the 100k world, but we do not have direct observations of atmospheric greenhouse gases from this period. Our results confirm that the amplitudes of glacial–interglacial variations in atmospheric greenhouse gases and Antarctic climate were reduced in the 40k world, and that the transition from the 40k to the 100k world was accompanied by a decline in minimum carbon dioxide concentrations during glacial maxima.

A 100-kiloparsec wind feeding the circumgalactic medium of a massive compact galaxy

一股100千帕秒的風吹向一個巨大、緊湊星系的環繞星系介質

作者：David S. N. Rupke, Alison Coil, James E. Geach, et al

鏈接：

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1686-1

摘要：

90%的重子位於星系外，要麼在星系周圍，要麼在星系間。理論指出銀河風是豐富和巨大的星系外介質的主要來源。

人們觀察到，緻密星爆產生的風，其流動距離略大於10千帕秒，但環繞星系的介質通常延伸超過100千帕秒。

這裡，我們報告了大而緊湊的SDSS星系J211824.06+001729.4的光學積分場觀測結果。

波長為3726埃和3729埃的氧[O II]譜線顯示出一個電離出流，跨度為80 * 100平方千秒差距，且在一個沙漏形狀的星雲中沉積了富含金屬的氣體。該星雲類似於一個真空的、被照亮的雙極氣泡。

Abstract

Ninety per cent of baryons are located outside galaxies, either in the circumgalactic orintergalactic medium. Theory points to galactic winds as the primary source of the enriched and massive circumgalactic medium. Winds from compact starbursts have been observed to flow to distances somewhat greater than ten kiloparsecs, but the circumgalactic medium typically extends beyond a hundred kiloparsecs. Here we report optical integral field observations of the massive but compact galaxy SDSS J211824.06+001729.4. The oxygen [O II] lines at wavelengths of 3726and 3729 angstroms reveal an ionized outflow spanning 80 by 100 square kiloparsecs, depositing metal-enriched gas at 10,000 kelvin through an hourglass-shaped nebula that resembles an evacuated and limb-brightened bipolar bubble.
