The adoption shall be handled according to the procedures of application, examination and registration.

1. Application: the party who requests the adoption of a child shall apply for adoption in person at the adoption registration authority and submit the relevant documents and supporting documents in accordance with the regulations.

2. 3. Registration: if the applicant meets the requirements set forth in the adoption law after verification, the registration shall be granted and an adoption registration certificate shall be issued. The adoption relationship shall be established as of the date of registration.

If a child is adopted in violation of the adoption law of the People's Republic of China, social maintenance fees shall be levied on the adopter according to what standards

1. Adoptions that meet the legal requirements but fail to be registered shall be levied at a rate of 1,000 yuan;

2. Adoptions without children but below the legal adoption age shall be levied at a rate of 2,000 yuan for each year in advance;

3. If there are children adopted, the number of children shall be levied according to the standard set forth in item (1) of article 46 of these regulations.
