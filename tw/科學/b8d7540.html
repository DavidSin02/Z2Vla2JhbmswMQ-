抗MCV抗體名字很長：抗突變瓜氨酸波形蛋白抗體

第一作者是來自意大利的，詳細信息如下


期刊簡介：


以下為摘要部分：

Abstract

摘要

Antiphospholipid antibodies (aPLs) are a heterogeneous group of autoantibodies essential for the diagnosis of antiphospholipid syndrome (APS) but do not predict clinical manifestations or disease progression. 抗磷脂抗體（aPLs）是一組包含有各種特異性的自身抗體的組合，它們是診斷抗磷脂綜合徵（APS)所必需的實驗室檢查特徵，但不能作為預測臨床表現或疾病進展的指標。

Hence, the co-presence of other antibodies may prove useful.

因此，血清中檢測到同時有其它抗體存在時，可能更有助於預測臨床表現或疾病進展。

Autoimmunity directed toward vimentin and other citrullinated peptides was established in rheumatoid arthritis (RA) and in orther autoimnume conditions including systemic lupus erytliemalosus (SLE).

在風溼性關節炎和其它包括系統性紅斑狼瘡（SLE）的自身免疫疾病中，針對波形蛋白和其它瓜氨酸化肽的自身免疫已建立。

We have previously described the presence of autoantibodies directed against vimentin/cardiolipin complex in patients with antiphospholipid syndrome (APS), but there are no data on the role of citrullinated vimentin in APS.

我們先前曾闡述過抗磷脂綜合徵患者血清中可檢測到抗波形蛋白/心磷脂複合物的自身抗體，但是並沒有關於瓜氨酸波形蛋白在APS患者中起什麼作用的研究資料。

Thus, we evaluated the prevalence and clinical significance of anti- MCV in APS patients.

因此，我們評估了抗MCV抗體在APS患者中的發生率和臨床意義。

材料和方法

The study group consisted of 79 unselected outpatients with APS. 實驗組納入了79名隨機挑選的患有APS的門診病人。

Control groups included 25 patients with SLE, 30 patients with RA, and 20 healthy subjects age- and sex-matched.

對照組包含25名患有SLE的患者，30名患RA的患者和20名年齡和性別匹配的健康受試者。

To detect anti-MCV,anti-vimentin, anti-vimentin/ cardiolipin, and anti-CCP2 antibodies, commercial or homemade enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) were performed.

採用商業或自制酶聯免疫吸附試劑盒（ELISA）來檢測抗MCV，抗波形蛋白，抗波形蛋白/心磷脂，和抗CCP2抗體。

結果：

Anti-MCV antibodies were found in a high percentage of APS patients (26.6%). APS患者的血清中檢測到抗MCV抗體佔比很高，為26.6%。

A significant correlation between anti-MCVand anti-vimentin/cardiolipin serum levels was observed (p = 0.029). 血清中抗MCV和抗波形蛋白/心磷脂水平之間存在顯著相關性(p=0.029)。

Moreover, vimentin reactivity was increased by its citnallination or conjugation with cardiolipin (p = 0.01 andp < 0.001, respectively). 此外，波形蛋白的檸檬酸化或與心磷脂的結合增加了波形蛋白的反應性（分別為p = 0.01和p <0.001）。

Interestingly, anti-MCV was found associated with the presence of arthritis (p = 0.011) and anti-vimentin/cardiolipin was highly specific for the presence of arterial or venous thrombosis in APS (p = 0.003 and p = 0.002. respectively). 有趣的是，抗MCV抗體與關節炎疾病有相關性（p=0.011），抗波形蛋白/心磷脂與APS患者發生動靜脈血栓有高度特異性（分別為p=0.003和p=0.002）。

結論：

The detection of additional autoantibodies may contribute to clinical assessment of APS patients. Citrullination may occur in APS and play a role in the pathogenesis of this condition.

其他自身抗體的檢測可能有助於APS患者的臨床評估。抗磷脂綜合徵中的患者血清中可能會檢測到瓜氨酸，瓜氨酸的產生在抗磷脂綜合徵的發病機理中起一定的作用。
