近視防控-近視背後的科學：視網膜追像生長(the eye grows)雙語

2020-10-29
| 科學

​The first reported case of myopia in experimental animals was observed serendipitously in non-human primates, during investigations into the cortical effects of monocular visual deprivation in young macaques. In these animals, sewing the eyelids shut for many months resulted in high levels of myopia; the longer the period of visual deprivation, the greater the resultant refractive error. Instead of sewing the eyelids shut, scientists now use frosted plastic goggles to induce deprivation myopia, or lenses with a strong negative power to cause “lens-induced myopia” (Figure 5). Today, the most commonly-used myopia model animals are chicks and guinea pigs.

在研究幼年獼猴單眼視覺剝奪的效應時，在非人類的靈長類動物中偶然發現了首例報道的近視眼的實驗動物。在這些動物中，將眼瞼縫合數月會導致高度近視，視覺剝奪的時間越長，產生的屈光不正越大。現在，科學家不縫合眼瞼，而是使用磨砂的塑料護目鏡來誘發剝奪性近視，或者使用具有強負屈光力的鏡片來引起“鏡片引起的近視”（圖5）。現在最常用的近視模型動物是小雞和豚鼠。

Figure 5. The effects of goggles on the initially focused image of a square-wave grating. When no goggle is applied, the grating image appears as sharply defined black and white lines (a). Myopia can be induced by manipulating retinal images optically, in animal models such as the chick, by application of a frosted diffuser goggle (b) or a negative-powered lens (c). Both treatments reduce spatial contrast of the image (difference between white and black), especially at higher spatial frequencies (the sharpness of the line borders).

圖5.護目鏡對方波光柵最初聚焦圖像的影響。當未應用任何護目鏡時，光柵圖像顯示為清晰的黑白線（a）。 在動物模型（如小雞）中，通過應用磨砂的散光鏡（b）或負片鏡（c），可以通過光學方式影響視網膜圖像來誘發近視。 兩種處理都降低了圖像的空間對比度（白色和黑色之間的差異），尤其是在較高的空間頻率下（線條邊界的清晰度）。

That we can experimentally-induce myopia in young, rapidly growing animals tells us that the visual environment plays an important role in regulation of eye growth. Affixing a frosted goggle or negative lens over the eye causes excessive axial elongation of the eye, and myopia, but affixing a positive lens over the eye causes inhibition of axial elongation, and far-sightedness (hyperopia) (Figure 6).

通過實驗來誘發快速成長的幼小動物的近視，揭示了視覺環境在調節眼球生長中起著重要的作用。在眼睛上貼上磨砂的護目鏡或負透鏡會導致眼軸過度伸長而近視，但在眼睛上貼正透鏡會導致軸向伸長受到抑制而遠視（圖6）。

Figure 6. Affixing a negative (concave or diverging) lens over the eye pushes the focal plane of the visual image behind the retina, inducing an increase in axial elongation and a more myopic refraction (a). Conversely, affixing a positive (convex or converging) lens over the eye pushes the focal plane of the visual image in front of the retina, inducing a decrease in axial elongation and a more hyperopic refraction (b).

圖6.在眼睛上固定負（凹或發散）透鏡將視覺圖像的焦平面推向視網膜後方，從而導致軸向伸長率增加和近視度數增加（a）。相反，將正（凸或會聚）透鏡固定在眼睛上會將視覺圖像的焦平面拉向視網膜前方，從而導致軸向伸長率降低和遠視屈光（b）。

Myopia-inducing stimuli require prolonged exposures (days-weeks) for a significant effect. In the chick, however, it takes only ≥2 hrs per day of unobstructed viewing of a richly textured visual environment to completely block the development of form-deprivation myopia , and positive-lens wear for only 2 min, four times per day, is enough to block lens-induced myopia. In addition, the eye can “recover” from experimentally induced myopia and return to an emmetropic refractive state, if the animal (or human) is young enough. Once an organism has reached adulthood, however, there is no known way to decrease the size of the globe, without surgical intervention.

引起近視的刺激物需要長時間暴露(數天-數週)才能產生顯著效果。但在小雞中，每天只要≥2小時不受干擾地觀察豐富的視覺環境，就能完全阻止視覺剝奪型近視的發展，而每天四次佩戴正鏡僅2分鐘，就足以阻止透鏡誘發的近視。此外，如果動物(或人類)足夠年輕，眼睛可以從實驗誘導的近視中“恢復”，並恢復到正常的屈光狀態。如果到了成年，沒有外科手術干預，就無法縮小眼球的大小。

  • visual deprivation 視覺剝奪

If the images on the retina are obscured by diffusers or lid suture, eyes elongate and form-deprivation myopia results in all species studied. Because no images are brought into focus by the excessive ocular elongation, it continues as long as vision is obscured. This response implies that image quality is normally involved in restraining eye growth.

如果視網膜上的圖像被散光鏡或眼瞼縫合所遮蔽，那麼所有被研究物種的眼球都會被拉長而近視。由於過度的眼部拉長導致圖像不能聚焦，只要視線被遮擋，這種情況就會持續。這種現象意味著圖像質量通常影響著眼球的生長。
