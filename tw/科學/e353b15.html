會議介紹

2018年深圳國際康復論壇在多方面領跑國內行業，論壇開辦的宗旨是聚焦康復領域的研究探索與實踐創新，實現不同學科、不同國界之間的交流合作，推動全球康復與健康事業的蓬勃發展。會議將邀請國內外康復醫療領域的知名專家、各康復醫院院長、各康復科主任、康復醫療護理人員等，將開展重症康復、促醒康復、醫養融合、哈佛論壇等14個分論壇，將圍繞當前的康復臨床技術、診療規範以及相關產業的熱門話題展開探討。

此次會議免註冊費並提供簡餐，授予1類學分4分。

Daniel

T. Meninger is the Director of Programs at Spaulding Rehabilitation

Hospital in Charlestown Massachusetts. Daniel

graduated from Boston University in 1999 with a Bachelor of Science

in Health Studies and in 2001 with a Master of Science in Physical

Therapy. In 2001, he joined Spaulding as a staff

Physical Therapist on the Cardiopulmonary Program and advanced to

Rehabilitation Coordinator in 2004. His role

changed to that of Physical Therapy Practice Leader when he assumed

the oversight of multiple programs before being promoted to Program

Director for the Spinal Cord Injury Program in

2012. His oversight expanded to include the Brain

Injury Program and most recently for the Director of Programs at

Spaulding Rehabilitation Hospital. In his role,

he is responsible for ensuring excellent patient care, monitoring

and improving patient outcomes and implementing process and quality

improvement initiatives. He also works to

maintain relationships with referring hospitals and other

facilities within the Spaulding Rehabilitation Network helping to

ensure seamless transitions of patients through the healthcare

continuum.

大會日程

2018年深圳國際康復論壇

2018年8月17-19日，2018年深圳國際康復論壇將在中國深圳深圳富臨大酒店舉行。本次會議主題“融合臨床·推動康復發展”。在此，誠邀國內外康復領域各位老師赴深圳分享康復學術熱點話題，一同探討臨床實踐。
