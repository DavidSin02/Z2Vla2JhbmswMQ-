皮球兄弟和七隻雞

2020-10-29
| 科技

昏暗的月光下，嗆鼻的灰塵味隨著夜風飄散。大門虛掩的莊園內，寂靜得恍如空城。園內參差不齊的青草、婆娑搖曳的樹影、肆意綻放的野花......看來，不見故人的莊園已經不是兄弟們當初玩耍的度假勝地了。曾經的美好已過去，無法淡忘美麗的回憶。因為愛你所以不願放棄，你時常出現在我夢裡。我還一直不斷在尋覓，多麼希望再次與你相遇，我親愛的皮皮，還有那七隻討厭的雞。小貓咪球球，幾次偷偷跑回故地卻不再見故人。



狗狗皮皮與貓咪球球，這對狗兄貓弟的故事如同加菲貓與狗狗歐迪一樣。在一起的時候總是為爭多吃一口食物，多躺一會兒小主人的懷抱，多幫女王叼送一次拖鞋......而爭得面紅耳赤、不可開交。後來，又不知道怎麼從一個電烤箱的蛋殼裡面，莫名其妙冒出來七隻小雞仔。本來皮球兄弟已經夠熱鬧的了，再加上這七隻雞，真的是上躥下跳，雞犬不寧，整個屋子就成了他們幾個的樂園。



女王愛狗狗，兄妹愛貓咪，老佛爺愛會下蛋的小母雞，只剩下蠶寶寶天天操心買各種狗糧、貓糧和雞食。長此以往，蠶寶寶是苦不堪言。雖然個個後臺都惹不起，但是良民逼急了也會揭竿起義，何況蠶寶寶還是位智慧的品質工程師。要知道女王愛皮皮，更愛自己的寶寶。老佛爺愛雞仔，也不想惹女王不高興。一番公關下來，就有了一次全家度假旅行。大大的度假村裡，熟睡中的皮皮狗哥哥和會下蛋的小母雞們莫名其妙就有了新的主人。度假結束，唯有兄妹偏愛的貓弟球球回家了，從此世界一下子安靜了。



這就是家庭寵物圈的品質管理，寸土寸金的大都市的家裡，養了這麼多的寵物，時間久了就是個麻煩，這是個不能迴避的問題。但是，家庭成員個個重要，個個都有需求，如何理解和對待他們的需求呢！還有，手心手背都是肉，如何才能讓大家開開心心接受這個變化呢？理清各個重要相關方的需求關係，在愉快的度假之旅中實施解決方案，完美！



質量就在我們身邊，生活如此，工作也是如此。高品質的做事方式就三個要點：識別關鍵問題，理解各相關方的利益訴求而制定合理方案，最後要用愉悅的方式執行方案。凡事都從這三個角度思考，智慧的品質工程師不僅能夠幫皮球兄弟和七隻小母雞找到新主人，而且還能有辦法隨時讓他們重聚，而且各方都非常愉悅哦！品質管理就是如此，靠的是智慧，不僅要做，還要做到點上，同時讓大家開心接受。

Under the dim moonlight, the pungent smell of dust wafted away on the night wind. The manor house, with its gates ajar, was as silent as an empty city. Its jagged grass, swaying trees, wild flowers in full bloom...It seemed that the manor was no longer the resort where the brothers had played. The beauty of the past is gone. We can't forget the beautiful memories. Because I love you so I don't want to give up, and you often appear in my dreams. I'm still looking for it. How I wish to see you again, my dear PP, and the seven nasty chickens. Kitten QQ ran back to the old place several times secretly but no longer see the players.

The story of PP and QQ is similar to that of Garfield and Odie. When they are together, they are always fighting for a mouthful of food, lying in the arms of the little master for a while more, to help the Queen hostess fetch a slipper... Later, out of the shell of an electronic oven born seven little chickens in my house. Originally PP and QQ were already enough lively. And now plus the seven chickens，they are too busy. The whole house became a paradise for them.

The Queen hostess loves dogs, siblings love cats, and Lagerfeld loves little hens that can lay eggs, so I take time to buy all kinds of dog food, cat food and chicken food every day. In the long run, me, silkworm is miserable. Although all of them have strong background, but the good people will also revolt, not to mention the Canbao host is a wise quality engineer. The Queen loves her babies more than PP. Lagerfeld loves chicken, but she doesn't want to upset the Queen. With a bit of negotiations, we had a family vacation. In the big holiday village, sleeping brother PP and little hens who lay eggs somehow got new owners. When the vacation was over, only the brother and sister's favorite cat QQ went home. The world was suddenly quiet.


This is the quality control of the family pet circle. Keeping so many pets in a metropolis where the apartment is limited and expensive is a long-term problem that cannot be avoided. However, each family member is important, each has needs, so how to understand and treat their needs? And how do you get people to be happy with this change? I sorted out the needs of key stakeholders and then implemented the solution during a pleasant vacation. Perfect!

Quality is all around us. So it is with life, and so it is with work. A high-quality way of doing things has three key points: identifying key problems, understanding the interests of all parties concerned and making reasonable plans, and finally implementing the plan in a pleasant way. We should think about everything from these three perspectives. The intelligent quality engineer can not only help the dog PP and the cat QQ, and seven little hens find new owners, but can also find a way to reunite them at any time, and all parties are very happy! That's what quality management is all about. We should not only have to do it, we should also do it right and make all parties happy.
