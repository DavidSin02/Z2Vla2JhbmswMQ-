Colorado River flow dwindles as warming-driven loss of reflective snow energizes evaporation

氣候變暖導致能反射陽光的積雪減少促進了蒸發致使科羅拉多河水流量減少

▲ 作者：P. C. D. Milly, K. A. Dunne

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1252


▲ 摘要：

河流流量對氣候系統變暖的敏感性是高度不確定的，而且我們對控制河流流量的過程瞭解甚少，這阻礙了河流對氣候變化的適應。一個突出的例子是科羅拉多河，那裡的氣象乾旱和氣候變暖正在使每年支持超過1萬億美元經濟活動的水資源減少。


利用輻射感知水文模型進行蒙特卡羅模擬，我們解決了靈敏度預估長期存在的巨大差異，揭示了水流控制的物理過程。


我們估計，徑流會在每增加一攝氏度時減少9.3％，主要因為雪量和隨之而來的對太陽輻射反射的減少帶來的蒸發量增加。預計的降水增加可能不足以完全抵消熱力學導致的乾旱。因此，嚴重缺水的風險將越來越大。


▲ Abstract

The sensitivity of river discharge to climate-system warming is highly uncertain, and the processes that govern river discharge are poorly understood, which impedes climate-change adaptation. A prominent exemplar is the Colorado River, where meteorological drought and warming are shrinking a water resource that supports more than 1 trillion dollars of economic activity per year. A Monte Carlo simulation with a radiation-aware hydrologic model resolves the longstanding, wide disparity in sensitivity estimates and reveals the controlling physical processes. We estimate that annual mean discharge has been decreasing by 9.3% per degree Celsius of warming because of increased evapotranspiration, mainly driven by snow loss and a consequent decrease in reflection of solar radiation. Projected precipitation increases likely will not suffice to fully counter the robust, thermodynamically induced drying. Thus, an increasing risk of severe water shortages is expected.


Persistent influence of obliquity on ice age terminations since the Middle Pleistocene transition

自中更新世過渡期以來，地球軌道傾斜對冰川末期的持續性影響

▲ 作者：Petra Bajo, Russell N. Drysdale, Jon D. Woodhead, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1235


▲ 摘要：

對過去64萬年冰川末期的同位素年齡測定表明，地球的氣候歲差在起作用，每一個冰川期和間冰期的週期約為4 ~ 5個2萬年。


然而，對更新世晚期的確切年齡估計的缺乏，使測試歲差持久性的嘗試陷入混亂。我們通過鈾鉛測年法確定了意大利洞穴堆積物的記錄，並將之與北大西洋的數據相結合。


結果顯示，過去10萬年中的頭兩個冰消期被兩次地球軌道傾斜週期分開，每次冰川末期都在同樣的傾斜高度開始，但歲差階段相反。對過去百萬年間的11個同位素年齡測定末期的評估表明，傾角不僅對冰川終止的起始，而且對終止的持續時間都有持續影響。


▲ Abstract

Radiometric dating of glacial terminations over the past 640,000 years suggests pacing by Earth’s climatic precession, with each glacial-interglacial period spanning four or five cycles of ~20,000 years. However, the lack of firm age estimates for older Pleistocene terminations confounds attempts to test the persistence of precession forcing. We combine an Italian speleothem record anchored by a uranium-lead chronology with North Atlantic ocean data to show that the first two deglaciations of the so-called 100,000-year world are separated by two obliquity cycles, with each termination starting at the same high phase of obliquity, but at opposing phases of precession. An assessment of 11 radiometrically dated terminations spanning the past million years suggests that obliquity exerted a persistent influence on not only their initiation but also their duration.

Enantioselective remote C–H activation directed by a chiral cation

手性陽離子對映選擇性遠程C-H活化

▲ 作者：Georgi R. Genov, James L. Douthwaite, Antti S. K. Lahdenperä, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1246


▲ 摘要：

手性陽離子作為有機催化劑已被廣泛應用，但其在過渡金屬催化反應中對映選擇性的應用還很少。儘管類似的電荷反轉策略已經取得了成功，即陽離子金屬配合物與手性陰離子配對。


我們在此報告一種策略，使一個共同的雙吡啶配體陰離子和它的銥配合物與一個衍生自奎寧的手性陽離子配對。


我們已經將這些離子配對的複合物應用於位於碳或磷中心的二芳基基序的去甲基化，通過對映選擇性的C-H硼化來實現長時間的不對稱誘導。原則上，許多常見類型的配體也可以採用這種方法。


▲ Abstract

Chiral cations have been used extensively as organocatalysts, but their application to rendering transition metal–catalyzed processes enantioselective remains rare. This is despite the success of the analogous charge-inverted strategy in which cationic metal complexes are paired with chiral anions. We report here a strategy to render a common bipyridine ligand anionic and pair its iridium complexes with a chiral cation derived from quinine. We have applied these ion-paired complexes to long-range asymmetric induction in the desymmetrization of the geminal diaryl motif, located on a carbon or phosphorus center, by enantioselective C–H borylation. In principle, numerous common classes of ligand could likewise be amenable to this approach.


Realization of the kagome spin ice state in a frustrated intermetallic compound

在阻挫金屬互化物中實現kagome自旋冰態

▲ 作者：Kan Zhao, Hao Deng, Hua Chen, Kate A. Ross, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1218


▲ 摘要：

自旋冰是一種奇異的物質相，其特徵是自旋受挫，這與水冰中的電偶極子類似。


在二維空間中，我們可以類似地定義kagome晶格上平面內類似的自旋冰規則。這些冰規則要求每個三角形板有一個單極子，可以導致不同類型的階數和激發。


利用實驗和理論方法，包括磁力測量、熱力學測量、中子散射和蒙特卡羅模擬，我們建立了HoAgGe作為一個晶體系統以實現kagome自旋冰態。


該系統具有多種部分有序和完全有序的狀態，以及一系列低溫場誘導相，所有這些都符合kagome冰規則。


▲ Abstract

Spin ices are exotic phases of matter characterized by frustrated spins obeying local “ice rules,” in analogy with the electric dipoles in water ice. In two dimensions, one can similarly define ice rules for in-plane Ising-like spins arranged on a kagome lattice. These ice rules require each triangle plaquette to have a single monopole and can lead to different types of orders and excitations. Using experimental and theoretical approaches including magnetometry, thermodynamic measurements, neutron scattering, and Monte Carlo simulations, we establish HoAgGe as a crystalline (i.e., nonartificial) system that realizes the kagome spin ice state. The system features a variety of partially and fully ordered states and a sequence of field-induced phases at low temperatures, all consistent with the kagome ice rule.


物理學Physics


Quantum-nondemolition state detection and spectroscopy of single trapped molecules

單捕獲分子的量子非破壞性態檢測與光譜分析

▲ 作者：Mudit Sinhal, Ziv Meir, Kaveh Najafian, Gregor Hegi, Stefan Willitsch

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1213


▲ 摘要：

捕獲的原子和離子是控制最好的量子系統之一，在量子科學中得到了廣泛的應用。對於分子來說，由於其複雜的能級結構，目前缺乏類似程度的控制。


原子離子作為分子離子探針的量子邏輯協議是實現這一水平控制的一個有希望的途徑，特別是對與黑體輻射解耦的同核種類。


在此，我們演示了一個針對單捕獲帶電氮分子的量子非破壞性協議。用>99%保真度檢測分子的自旋振動態，在不破壞量子態的情況下測量光譜的躍遷。該方法為分子光譜學、務態化學和分子量子位的實現提供了新方法。


▲ Abstract

Trapped atoms and ions, which are among the best-controlled quantum systems, find widespread applications in quantum science. For molecules, a similar degree of control is currently lacking owing to their complex energy-level structure. Quantum-logic protocols in which atomic ions serve as probes for molecular ions are a promising route for achieving this level of control, especially for homonuclear species that decouple from blackbody radiation. Here, a quantum-nondemolition protocol on single trapped N2+ molecules is demonstrated. The spin-rovibronic state of the molecule is detected with >99% fidelity, and a spectroscopic transition is measured without destroying the quantum state. This method lays the foundations for new approaches to molecular spectroscopy, state-to-state chemistry, and the implementation of molecular qubits.


天文學Astronomy


Ammonium salts are a reservoir of nitrogen on a cometary nucleus and possibly on some asteroids

銨鹽是彗星核和一些小行星上的氮儲藏庫

▲ 作者：Olivier Poch, Istiqomah Istiqomah, Eric Quirico, Pierre Beck, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/eaaw7462


▲ 摘要：

在彗星中測量到的氮碳比低於太陽，如果彗星中存在未知的氮庫，這種差異可以得到緩解。


彗星67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko的彗核的表面上存在一個約3.2微米的未知的廣譜反射率特徵。在實驗室實驗的基礎上，我們認為這一吸收帶是由表面混入粉塵的銨鹽引起的。


吸收帶的深度表明，半揮發性銨鹽是彗星中氮的一個重要儲集層，可能主導難降解有機物和揮發性更強的物質。類似的吸收特徵出現在一些小行星的光譜中，暗示著小行星、彗星和母星際雲之間的成分聯繫。


▲ Abstract

The measured nitrogen-to-carbon ratio in comets is lower than for the Sun, a discrepancy which could be alleviated if there is an unknown reservoir of nitrogen in comets. The nucleus of comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko exhibits an unidentified broad spectral reflectance feature around 3.2 micrometers, which is ubiquitous across its surface. On the basis of laboratory experiments, we attribute this absorption band to ammonium salts mixed with dust on the surface. The depth of the band indicates that semivolatile ammonium salts are a substantial reservoir of nitrogen in the comet, potentially dominating over refractory organic matter and more volatile species. Similar absorption features appear in the spectra of some asteroids, implying a compositional link between asteroids, comets, and the parent interstellar cloud.


醫學Medicine


Structure of CD20 in complex with the therapeutic monoclonal antibody rituximab

CD20與治療性單克隆抗體利妥昔單抗的複合物結構

▲ 作者：Lionel Rougé, Nancy Chiang, Micah Steffek, Christine Kugel, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/1224


▲ 摘要：

CD20是B細胞的特異性標記物，是治療惡性腫瘤和自身免疫紊亂的單克隆抗體的熱門靶點，但其結構和功能尚不清楚。利妥昔單抗（RTX）在臨床上已使用了20年，但我們對它是如何激活補體殺死B細胞的依然知之甚少。


我們得到了CD20與RTX的複合物結構，並且發現CD20呈現的是結合了2個RTX抗原結合片段（Fabs）的緊密二聚體結構，每個片段包含一個複合表位和一個廣泛的同型Fab: Fab斷片。


我們的數據表明，RTX將CD20交聯到環形組件中，從而形成了補體補充的結構模型。我們的結果進一步強調了同型Fab:Fab相互作用在靶向寡聚細胞表面標記物方面的潛在相關性。


▲ Abstract

Cluster of differentiation 20 (CD20) is a B cell membrane protein that is targeted by monoclonal antibodies for the treatment of malignancies and autoimmune disorders but whose structure and function are unknown. Rituximab (RTX) has been in clinical use for two decades, but how it activates complement to kill B cells remains poorly understood. We obtained a structure of CD20 in complex with RTX, revealing CD20 as a compact double-barrel dimer bound by two RTX antigen-binding fragments (Fabs), each of which engages a composite epitope and an extensive homotypic Fab:Fab interface. Our data suggest that RTX cross-links CD20 into circular assemblies and lead to a structural model for complement recruitment. Our results further highlight the potential relevance of homotypic Fab:Fab interactions in targeting oligomeric cell-surface markers.


Liquid-liquid phase separation drives skin barrier formation

液液相分離驅動皮膚屏障的形成

▲ 作者：Felipe Garcia Quiroz, Vincent F. Fiore, John Levorse, Lisa Polak, et al.

▲ 鏈接：

https://science.sciencemag.org/content/367/6483/eaax9554


▲ 摘要：

在體表，皮膚的復層扁平上皮受到極端環境的挑戰。皮膚表面由去細胞核的、扁平的體表鱗屑組成。它們來源於潛在的角質形成細胞，這些細胞顯示含有絲狀蛋白的角化透明蛋白顆粒（KGs），其功能尚不清楚。


在這裡，我們發現聚絲蛋白通過液液相分離來組裝KGs。相分離的動態支配著終末分化，並被人類皮膚屏障疾病相關突變所破壞。


我們使用熒光傳感器來研究小鼠的內源性相位行為。表皮分層過程中的相變使細胞空間擁擠著液體狀的KGs，其聚結受到角蛋白絲束的束縛。


我們對靠近皮膚表面的細胞進行了成像，發現環境調節的KG相動力學推動了鱗屑的形成。因此，表皮結構和功能是由相分離動力學驅動的。


▲ Abstract

At the body surface, skin’s stratified squamous epithelium is challenged by environmental extremes. The surface of the skin is composed of enucleated, flattened surface squames. They derive from underlying, transcriptionally active keratinocytes that display filaggrin-containing keratohyalin granules (KGs) whose function is unclear. Here, we found that filaggrin assembles KGs through liquid-liquid phase separation. The dynamics of phase separation governed terminal differentiation and were disrupted by human skin barrier disease–associated mutations. We used fluorescent sensors to investigate endogenous phase behavior in mice. Phase transitions during epidermal stratification crowded cellular spaces with liquid-like KGs whose coalescence was restricted by keratin filament bundles. We imaged cells as they neared the skin surface and found that environmentally regulated KG phase dynamics drive squame formation. Thus, epidermal structure and function are driven by phase-separation dynamics.
