光吸收能量嗎？

2020-10-29
| 科技

光子作為能量本身不吸收其他能量。比如你無法加熱單一光子或一束光線。實際上光子連被截停或俘獲都是不可能的，就更別提加熱了。因為光子不帶電荷因此無法被磁場束縛; 光子也不能被物質實體束縛，因為光子作為能量一定會被物質吸收。假設一個密閉盒子中，一個火柴持續發出10秒鐘的光芒然後熄滅，這些光子最終都會被盒子的邊緣物質作為能量吸收，盒內壁表現為溫度升高。在普朗克尺度下解釋的話，那麼盒內邊緣原子的核外電子因為俘獲光子而發生電子躍遷。


即便不考慮上述光子不能被束縛的條件，現在我們假設如果光可以吸收其他能量的話，那麼光譜中各個波段顏色的光就會相互吸收能量，最後就會融合成只有一個波段（顏色）了。如果只考慮可見光波段（400～700納米），想象一下白色的太陽光譜中的紫、藍、青、綠、黃、橙、紅等顏色相互吸收彼此波段的能量後，紫、藍、青等高階能量光波會因為損失能量使得自身光波變長，進而顏色偏向紅色波段；而黃、橙、紅等低能量光波會因為吸收了高階能量使得自身光波變短進而顏色更偏向藍色，最後的融合顏色應該是綠光（可見光譜400～700納米波長的中間值）。如果把不可見光部分也考慮進來，則高能光如伽馬射線、X光、紫外線會不斷損失自身能量從而喪失放射性。而無線電波和長波等低能量不可見光將可能會因為獲得能量而變成可見光。但是顯然，我們從未觀測到上述現象，就像我們知道陽光被三稜鏡分解成為不同顏色的光後不會因為相互間吸收能量而再重新合成為單一顏色一樣，光作為能量是不能再吸收其他形式的能量的。


如果你對以上的解釋感到困惑，沒關係，你還可以做這樣一個簡單的思想實驗：你有紅光和綠光兩個手電筒交叉照射呈X形狀，在交叉部分由於光波干涉你會看到黃光，但交叉後的光線卻依舊是紅光和綠光，且交叉後的紅光和綠光彼此並沒有因為獲得額外的能量而使得自己的波長變短進而更偏向藍色波段。

Light as energy, does not absorb other energy. You can not heat up a photon or a beam of light. In fact, it is impossible to even constrain a single photon, not to mention heating it up. Because photon carries no electronic charge and this makes it impossible to be constrained by any electromagnetic field; photon as energy will be absorbed by matter. In a sealed box made of matter, a matchstick gives 10 second light and then go off, all those photons will be taken as energy by atoms in the inner edge of the box, the temperature of which will rise. Interpretation in Plunk level is that the electrons of the edge atoms will quantum lead to higher orbit.


Even if we do not consider the above constraint problem, now let’s imagine light can absorb other energy, then the different colour wavelength from the light spectrum will absorb energy with each other, which will ultimately merge into only one single wavelength (colour). In the visible light range (400 ~700 nanometre), Imagine that all the colours of light (violet, blue, cyan, green, yellow, orange and red) absorb energy with each other, the high energy light (violet and blue) will lose energy to lower energy light (yellow, orange and red) and shift themselves to longer wavelength; While on the other hand, the lower energy photons will acquire those energy passed from shorter wavelength (violet, blue light) and get themselves into shorter wavelength, then will finally merge to green light (medium value in visible spectrum of 400~700 nanometre). If we extend this idea to invisible light, then imaginably the gamma ray, X ray and ultra violet will lose their radioactive nature and become visible light when they lose their energy, and those low energy invisible light like infrared wave will become visible because they absorb energy from higher wave length. Back to real, we never observed such phenomenon, just like we expect to see different colours of light when we put a prism in front of the sun light and we know those colours will not merge into a single colour. Light as energy can not absorb other energy form.


If you are confused with the above, don’t worry, you can think like this: You cross-fire 2 beams of flash light (red and green) forming X shape, you will see a yellow light in the cross area, after the cross section, they are still red and green light, and those red and green light after crossing are not getting any bluer or perform any shorter wavelength because they didn’t absorb each others’ energy at all.
