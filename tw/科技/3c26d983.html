負Keldysh準概率的非經典行為證明

Potts引入了一個實驗測試，來排除在使用單個檢測器在任意時間測量任意可觀測量時獲得的統計數據的經典解釋。該測試需要對測量值有一定的信任度，表現為對探測器的一些自然的假設。在量子理論中，馮·諾依曼測量很好地描述了作者考慮的情形。它們可以自然地用Keldysh準概率分佈（KQPD）和測量儀器施加的不精確性和反作用來描述。作者發現，當且僅當KQPD顯示負值時，才能從測量數據中排除經典描述。作者提供了基於模擬數據的例子，考慮了有限數量統計的影響。除了提供用於保證非經典性的實驗工具之外，他的結果還提供了負的準概率分佈（比如Wigner函數和全計數統計）的非經典性質的操作含義。（崔石峰）

避免不可逆性：

量子資源的共振轉換操控

We identify and explore the intriguing property of resource resonance arising within resource theories of entanglement, coherence, and thermodynamics. While the theories considered are reversible asymptotically, the same is generally not true in realistic scenarios where the available resources are bounded. The finite-size effects responsible for this irreversibility could potentially prohibit small quantum information processors or thermal machines from achieving their full potential. Nevertheless, we show here that by carefully engineering the resource interconversion process any such losses can be greatly suppressed. Our results are predicted by higher order expansions of the trade-off between the rate of resource interconversion and the achieved fidelity, and are verified by exact numerical optimizations of the appropriate underlying approximate majorization conditions.

驅動的二階非線性光子晶格的量子臨界區

We study an array of coupled optical cavities in the presence of two-photon driving and dissipation. The system displays a critical behavior similar to that of a quantum Ising model at finite temperature. Using the corner-space renormalization method, we compute the steady-state properties of finite lattices of varying size, both in one and two dimensions. From a finite-size scaling of the average of the photon number parity, we highlight the emergence of a critical point in regimes of small dissipations, belonging to the quantum Ising universality class. For increasing photon loss rates, a departure from this universal behavior signals the onset of a quantum critical regime, where classical fluctuations induced by losses compete with long-range quantum correlations.

We address the nature of phase transitions in periodically driven systems coupled to a bath. The latter enables asynchronized nonequilibrium Floquet steady state at finite entropy, which weanalyze for rapid drives within a nonequilibrium renormalization group (RG) approach. While the infinitely rapidly driven limit exhibits a second-orderphase transition, here we reveal that fluctuations turn the transition firstorder when the driving frequency is finite. This can be traced back to a universal mechanism, which crucially hinges on the competition of degenerate, near critical modes associated with higher Floquet Brillouin zones. Thecritical exponents of the infinitely rapidly driven system—including a new, independent one—can yet be probed experimentally upon smoothly tuning towards that limit.

熱漲落和相互作用可以產生拓撲保護態

拓撲的量子和經典材料都可以表現出對抗無序的魯棒性，例如，非相互作用的粒子和線性波都具有這種特性。Pedro等人演示瞭如何構建拓撲保護態，這些態是由軟物質或微型力學結構固有的強相互作用和熱漲落的結合產生的。他們考慮了受一類空間調製基底電位影響的張力作用下的波動線(如聚合物或渦旋線)。在平衡態下，這些線趨向於整體傾斜，傾斜度與整數拓撲不變量即Chern數成比例。正如經典Langevin動力學模擬所證實的，這種量子化傾斜對於基底無序是魯棒的。這種魯棒性的產生是因為該系統中的熱波動線的激發由於線間相互作用而產生能隙。他們利用一個映射建立了這個模式的拓撲基礎，這個映射的元素分別對應相互作用線系統和迄今為止尚未探索的可推廣到虛時間的Thouless泵浦。他們的工作指向一類新的經典拓撲現象，其拓撲特徵表現為在有限溫度下而不是傳輸測量中觀察到的結構性質。（王赫）
