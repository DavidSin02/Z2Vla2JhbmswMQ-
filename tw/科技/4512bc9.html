My Elder Brother

My elder brother is 16 years sld,He studies at NO. 5 Middle School.That is a very good middle school.All the students there are excellent and study very well. My brother is excellent,too.He does well in maths, physics and English. He says he would like to become a scientist in the future.

My elder brother is also a good player.He is good at playing football,so he is chosen in the school football team. He always plays well in the games. He also likes to help others,so his teachers and classmates all like him.

我的哥哥

我的哥哥16歲了，他在五中學習，那是一所非常好的學校，那裡的學生都很棒，學習都很好。我的哥哥也很出色。他的數學、物理和英語學得都很好，他說將來要當一名科學家。

我的哥哥還是個運動健將。因為他足球踢得很好，所以被吸收加入了學校的足球隊。他在比賽中總是表現得很出色。他還喜歡幫助他人，所以，他的老師和同學們都很喜歡他。
