整理英語怎麼讀：

Arrange 釋義：安排;籌備;整理;排列;佈置;譜寫，改編

音標：英 [əˈreɪndʒ] 美 [əˈreɪndʒ]


put in order 釋義：飭;整理，檢修

音標：英 [pʊt ɪn ˈɔːdə(r)] 美 [pʊt ɪn ˈɔːrdər]


Reorganize 釋義：組織調整;重組化原則;重組化;改組;再整合

音標:英 [riˈɔːɡənaɪz] 美 [riˈɔːrɡənaɪz]


sort out 釋義：把…分類;整理;解決（問題）;理清（細節）;

音標：英 [ˈsɔːt aʊt] 美 [ˈsɔːrt aʊt]


整理英語怎麼說：

1、When she has a little spare time she enjoys arranging dried flowers.

她稍有空閒便喜歡擺弄乾花。

2、This time it was a friend ringing to try to arrange a fishing trip in Scotland.

這次是一個朋友打來電話，想組織一次到蘇格蘭的釣魚旅行。


3、The hotel manager will arrange for a babysitter.

賓館經理會安排人照看嬰兒。


4、I've arranged your hotels for you.

我已為你們安排好了旅館。


5、He started to arrange the books in piles.

他開始將書成堆擺放。


6、We can arrange this for a small charge.

我們可以將此安排好，只收取很少的費用。


7、Pour a pool of sauce on two plates and arrange the meat neatly.

在兩個盤子裡倒少許沙司醬，然後把肉擺放整齊。


8、This material points to the truth that any co-operative in a state of confusion can be put in order.

這個材料指出了一個真理，就是任何情況混亂的合作社，都是可以整理的。


9、Things have to be put in order here before they come.

在他們來之前，這一切都得要整理好。


10、Branch of various civil administration is comprehensive and normative system of small custodial put in order.

各級民政部門全面規範了低保管理制度。


11、Document which have to is put in order.

入檔文件，必須放置有序的文件。


12、It is the mother who is expected to reorganize her busy schedule.

應該讓母親來重新安排自己緊張的日程。


13、I think it is grinded over, it should be gave room for people to imagine and Reorganize.

我覺得磨得過分了，應給後來的人想像和整理的空間。


14、In1982, three member families and the Secretary met to reorganize.

1982年，三個成員家庭和祕書長會見了重組。


15、To convert the address book to XML, you need to reorganize some of the data.

要將地址簿轉換成XML，需要對一些數據重新組織。


16、Further understand and realize the activities they have carried out and reorganize their knowledge structure.

對自己做過的活動進一步理解和認識，重組自己的知識結構。


17、Once you create a component, you cannot reorganize it into sub components.

一旦創建了一個構件，你不能重新組織它到子構件裡。


18、In doing so the proxy can monitor requests to the real object and filter or reorganize them if needed.

這樣，代理可以監控對實際對象的請求，並在過濾請求和需要請求的情況下重新組織這些請求。


19、You can reorganize specific tables.

您可以重組特定表。


20、I like to clean out the closets and reorganize them once a year.

我喜歡每年一次清理一下衣櫃和重新擺放衣物。
