黃金圈：焦糖核桃蛋糕

直接切開給你看斷面層：

這個模具使環形硅膠模，一套模具有兩個，一個大環一個小環，這個蛋糕的做法簡單來說就是大環形硅膠模內放入慕斯，再放入小硅膠模製作的“夾心”，然後冷凍淋面裝飾就ok了，呃...也別小瞧它，口感是無敵的——chef Kevin Gully的獲獎作品哦

焦糖淋面

150克 水

200克 葡萄糖漿（A）

300克 細砂糖

100克 葡萄糖漿 （B）

200克 煉乳

160克 吉利丁液（22克吉利丁粉+138克冷水）

280克 嘉利寶黃金巧克力 30.4%

20克 嘉利寶Arriba牛奶巧克力 39%

150克 透明中性鏡面果膠

製作：

1、水和200克葡萄糖漿A在厚底平底鍋內加熱，期間適當攪動，煮熱後離火靜置待用。

2、在另一個平底鍋中加熱細砂糖和100克葡萄糖漿B，並適當用膠刮刀攪動，當焦糖溫度達到180℃時，離火，緩慢衝入“步驟1”熱的液體，並用手持攪拌器（蛋抽）攪拌，期間要注意避免被大量的整齊燙傷。當全部加入後，倒入煉乳，然後重新加熱並快速煮沸。一旦煮沸後立刻離火併加入吉利丁液拌融。混合均勻後倒在裝有兩種巧克力的小盆中拌勻，再加入鏡面果膠，用手持均質機攪拌乳化至光滑細膩。保鮮膜貼面覆蓋冷藏隔夜後使用。

※注：加入煉乳後會稍呈分離狀，不過不用擔心靜置後自然會均勻。


無筋混合粉

300克 黑麥粉

100克 米粉

100克 玉米粉

製作：

1、全部混合在一起並用手持攪拌器拌勻，混合後過篩到烘焙紙上。

2、稱取45克用於下述的配方中，剩餘的密封用於其他配方。

※注：粉的用量太少，如果僅按所需的45克按比例稱取3種粉混合，則達不到所需效果，所以此處剩餘的可以密封待用於其他配方中。

焦糖海綿蛋糕

100克 細砂糖

40克 無鹽黃油

50克 沸水

70克 山核桃粉

45克 無筋混合粉（配方見上方）

50克 蛋黃

75克 蛋白

75克 轉化糖漿

50克 核桃碎（用於最後灑在蛋糕麵糊表面）

製作：

1、製作核桃粉：將500克左右的山核桃烤熟後用料理機研磨成粉，稱取140克待用，其他的稍後使用。

2、砂糖在厚底平底鍋中加熱至焦糖狀態，加入黃油拌融把那個開始緩慢加入沸水（注意小心產生的大量蒸汽）用手持攪拌器拌勻。

3、將核桃粉與“無筋混合粉”在盆中拌勻，倒入焦糖，拌勻，靜置幾分鐘待其冷卻至50℃左右（此溫度就不會燙熟後邊繼續加入的蛋黃了）。加入蛋黃攪拌均勻後靜置。

4、蛋白和轉化糖漿在攪拌機中用球槳（whisk attachment）低速攪拌為蛋白霜。將蛋白霜用分三次加入到焦糖/蛋黃中並用膠刮刀輕輕翻拌均勻。

5、裝入裱花袋內，每個模（Flexipan，直徑16cm）擠入90克，並在蛋糕麵糊上鋪擺山核桃仁。以170℃烘烤12分鐘，冷卻待用。

山核桃沙佈列

200克 無鹽黃油

140克 拉帕杜拉糖（紅糖）

240克 無筋混合粉（配方見上）

260克 山核桃粉

50克 可可脂（嘉利寶）

50克 33.6%牛奶巧克力

製作：

1、黃油微波爐軟化或室溫自然軟化後，在攪拌機中用扁槳（paddle attachment）與砂糖攪拌至光滑細膩的奶油狀。停止攪拌，加入核桃粉和無筋混合粉攪拌均勻（不要攪拌過度）。將一半的麵糊放在兩張烘焙紙之間擀壓至3.8cm厚度。冷凍至完全堅挺（約20分鐘，如果是速凍櫃則只需5分鐘），然後裁切為2個直徑16cm的圓形，再用在中心位置裁切掉7cm直徑的孔，呈圓環形。放在硅膠烤盤上以140℃烘烤30分鐘。（剩餘的保鮮膜密封冷藏用於其他配方中）。

2、將可可脂與巧克力混合融化（水浴或微波爐），輕輕塗刷在烤熟的沙佈列表面，冷藏或冷凍儲存(目的是防止慕斯層使沙佈列軟化)。

百香果果凍

160克 百香果果茸

80克 香蕉果茸

80克 芒果果茸

16克 百香果籽（新鮮的）

8克 玉米澱粉

32克 吉利丁液（4.5克吉利丁粉+27.5克冷水）

製作：

1、在厚底平底鍋中將香草（香草莢剖開刮籽）、百香果和玉米澱粉混合煮沸，期間適當攪拌，一旦開始沸騰，立刻離火倒入量杯中，加入吉利丁液並用手持均質機攪拌均勻。

2、將160克放入小一號的硅膠模具內。從冷卻的焦糖海綿蛋糕上裁切一個比模具稍小一點的圓環，放在果凍上邊（核桃的一面朝上）並輕壓使之緊密結合沒有空隙，冷凍1小時（或急凍15分鐘）。

※注：剩餘的海綿蛋糕可以密封冷凍用於其他配方或下次製作。


軟焦糖

100克 葡萄糖漿

60克 細砂糖

210克 35%淡奶油

2.5克 海鹽

55克 無鹽黃油

15克 可可脂

15克 30.4%黃金焦糖牛奶巧克力（嘉利寶）

製作：

1、葡萄糖漿在微波爐內稍加熱，然後放入厚底平底鍋中，加入砂糖，加熱至呈棕色（溫度達到180-185℃）。淡奶油也在微波爐內加熱約90秒。

2、緩慢將熱的淡奶油加入到焦糖中，要注意會產生大量蒸汽，攪拌至糖完全溶於奶油，加入鹽再次拌勻。倒入盆中降溫至50℃。加入黃金焦糖巧克力和切丁黃油用膠刮刀攪拌融化，再用手持均質機攪拌乳化至細膩。

3、將50克倒在冷凍的焦糖蛋糕上，晃動模具使焦糖醬均勻攤平在蛋糕上。冷凍。

焦糖奶油

125克 細砂糖（A）

250克 35%淡奶油

25克 細砂糖（B）

60克 蛋黃

38克 吉利丁液

125克 馬氏卡邦乳酪

製作：

1、將125克砂糖在厚底平底鍋中加熱為焦糖，同時將淡奶油在微波爐內加熱90秒。將熱的淡奶油加入到焦糖的鍋內並攪拌混勻，離火靜置。

2、將另外25克砂糖和蛋黃在小碗中攪拌，將熱的“步驟1”倒入一小部分並拌勻後再倒回平底鍋內加熱至82-85℃類似英式奶醬的狀態。注意切勿加熱過度，將之倒入小盆中加入吉利丁攪拌，當其完全融化後冷藏降溫至50℃（約耗時10-15分鐘）。當焦糖醬降溫後，加入馬氏卡邦乳酪用手持均質機攪拌均勻呈光滑細膩狀

3、取100克注入到冷凍的模具的焦糖上，繼續冷凍。

零陵香米布丁

100克 35%淡奶油

400克 全脂牛奶

3克 零陵香豆（1個）

25克 細砂糖

50克 大米（意大利艾保利奧稻米）

50克 30.4%黃金焦糖牛奶巧克力

製作：

1、將牛奶、淡奶油和磨碎的零陵香豆混合在一起密封浸泡24小時。然後過濾到厚底平底鍋中煮沸，期間適當攪拌，當其沸騰時，加入事先清洗的大米拌勻，煮10-15分鐘。

2、倒在小盆中的巧克力上並用膠刮刀攪拌後，倒在平盤內並用保鮮膜貼面覆蓋使之快速降溫，冷藏幾個小時。

3、當模具內的焦糖奶油凝結後，在其頂部再加入150克布丁，抹平整繼續冷凍。

山核桃慕斯

2個 香草莢

180克 全脂牛奶

40克 蛋黃

180克 30.4%黃金焦糖牛奶巧克力

25克 吉利丁液

50克 山核桃醬

301克 35%淡奶油

製作：

1、將香草莢剖開刮籽放入牛奶中密封浸泡隔夜。

2、放入厚底平底鍋中加熱，然後倒在打散的蛋黃上拌勻後再倒回鍋內重新煮至82-85℃，期間適當攪拌。

3、過濾到巧克力上，再加入吉利丁攪拌混合，加入核桃醬用手持均質機攪拌乳化至光滑細膩。降溫至34-35℃，將打發至軟尖峰狀態的稀奶油分2-3次加入，輕輕拌勻。

黃金圈

100克 菜籽油

50克 金色閃粉

製作：

混合用手持攪拌機拌勻，冷藏。


組裝完成

1、將冷凍完全的夾心脫模。

2、大的環形慕斯模具內(Silikomart Kit Lady Queen silicon mould)注入350剋核桃慕斯，放入冷凍的環形夾心（平面朝上），輕輕按壓使之完全浸入慕斯內，再將核桃酥脆片鋪上（塗刷了可可脂/巧克力的一面朝下）。

3、冷凍3-4小時完全凍結後，脫模，淋面，用慕斯鋼圈蘸一點黃金膏，印在慕斯淋面上，裝飾巧克力完成。

Caramel Pecan Entremet

by Kevin Gully

Caramel glaze

150g water

200g liquid glucose (A)

300g caster (superfine) sugar

100g liquid glucose (B)

200g evaporated milk

160g gelatine mass (1:6)

280g Callebaut gold couverture 30.4%

20g Callebaut Arriba milk couverture 39%

150g mirror glaze

METHOD

Heat the water and glucose (A) in a saucepan, whisking occasionally by hand. Once hot, remove the saucepan from the heat and set aside.

Heat the sugar and glucose (B) in a separate saucepan and stir occasionally with a spatula to make a caramel. When the caramel is ready (at 180 ˚C to 185˚C), remove from the heat and slowly pour in the hot water with the glucose in intervals, while whisking by hand. Be careful of the steam. When all the water has been added, pour in the evaporated milk. Return to the heat and bring to a quick boil. As it reaches a boil, remove from the heat and whisk in the gelatine mass. Once it is combined, pour it over the gold chocolate and milk chocolate in a bowl and whisk by hand to combine. Add the mirror glaze and emulsify with a stick blender until smooth. Please note, the glaze may split a little due to the condensed milk, but will retrieve itself when emulsifying. Cover with plastic wrap touching the surface of the glaze and leave in the fridge overnight.


Gluten free flour mix

300g buckwheat flour

100g rice flour

100g cornflour/ corn starch

METHOD

Place all the ingredients in a bowl and whisk together by hand. Once combined, sift onto a sheet of baking paper.

Weigh out 45g for the recipe below and store the remainder in an airtight container for another recipe.

※NOTE：The flour is tricky to make in smaller quantities, but you can use the leftover in another recipe.


Caramel sponge

100g caster (superfine) sugar

40g unsalted butter

50g hot water

70g pecan powder

45g gluten free flour mix (above)

50g egg yolks

75g egg white

75g inverted sugar

50g pecans, for sprinkling

METHOD

To create the pecan powder, roast some pecans in the oven and grind in a blender to create a powder. Weigh out 140g for this recipe and set the remaining aside for use later in the recipe.

Heat the sugar in a saucepan to make a caramel. Add the butter and slowly pour in the hot water, being careful of the hot steam. Whisk by hand to combine. Place the pecan powder and gluten free flour mix in a bowl and pour in the caramel, whisking together by hand. Allow to sit for a few minutes to cool (approximately 50˚C) so it doesn't cook the eggs. Add the egg yolks and continue to whisk by hand. Set aside. To create the meringue, add the fresh egg white and inverted sugar to the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment. Mix on a slow speed until it reaches a firm peak. Using a spatula, slowly fold the meringue into the caramel mixture in three intervals. Once combined, transfer to a piping bag fitted with a 12mm round piping tip. Line a baking tray with the Flexipan. Pipe 90g of the mixture into each crevice of the mould in a swirling motion. Arrange pecans around the edge of the sponge. Bake at 170˚C for 12 minutes. Allow to cool.


Pecan crunchy sable

200g unsalted butter

140g rapadura sugar

240g gluten free flour mix (above)

260g pecan powder

50g Callebaut Mycryo cocoa butter

50g Callebaut 823NV milk couverture 33.6%

METHOD

Soften the butter in a microwave and place in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment. Add in the sugar and mix until you achieve a smooth, creamy texture.

Stop the mixer and add in the pecan powder and gluten free flour mix. Mix to combine, but do not overmix. Remove the bowl from the mixer. Place half of the dough between two sheets of baking paper and roll out to 3.8mm with a rolling pin. You can also use a dough sheeter if you have one. Place in the freezer until cold, this will take approximately 20 minutes in a domestic freezer or 5 minutes in a blast freezer. Remove the baking paper and cut out two discs with a 16cm ring. Cut out a 7cm ring from the centre of the crunchy disc. Line a baking tray with a Silpain mat and place the discs on top. Bake at 140˚C for 30 minutes. Place the scraps of dough together, cover in plastic wrap and keep in the fridge for another recipe. Melt the cocoa butter and chocolate together in the microwave in 30 second increments. Carefully brush a thin layer of the mixture on top of the discs. Allow to set in the fridge or freezer.


Passion fruit felly

0.5u Heilala vanilla bean, split and scraped

160g Ravifruit passionfruit puree

80g Ravifruit banana puree

80g Ravifruit mango puree

16g fresh passionfruit seeds

8g cornflour/ corn starch

32g gelatine mass(1:6)

METHOD

Place the vanilla seeds, purees, fresh passionfruit and cornflour in a saucepan and bring to a boil, while whisking by hand. Ensure you don't cook it. Once it starts boiling, remove from the heat and transfer to a jug. Mix in the gelatine mass with a spatula before emulsifying with a stick blender.

Pour 160g of the jelly into the smaller insert of the silicon mould. Cut out a 9cm ring from the centre of the cooled caramel sponge. Place the larger ring of sponge on top of the passionfruit jelly (pecan side up) and press down slightly. Place in the domestic freezer for 1 hour or 15 minutes in the blast freezer. You can store any leftover sponge in the freezer to utilise in another recipe.


Soft caramel

100g liquid glucose

60g caster (superfine) sugar

210g fresh cream 35% fat

2.5g sea salt

55g unsalted butter

15g Callebaut Mycryo cocoa butter

15g Callebaut gold couverture 30.4%

METHOD

Heat the glucose in the microwave slightly then add to a saucepan. Add the sugar to the saucepan and heat until you achieve the desired colour of brown-light brown (the temperature should be between 180 ˚C and 185˚C). Heat the cream in the microwave for approximately 90 seconds (it will differ between microwaves, so ensure you check it).

Slowly add the hot cream to the caramel, being careful of the hot steam. Whisk by hand until the sugar has completely dissolved then add the salt and mix again to combine. Transfer the mixture to a jug and cool to 50˚C. Add the gold chocolate and cocoa butter and mix with a spatula before adding in the cubed butter. Emulsify with a stick blender until shiny and smooth.

Transfer the caramel to a depositor and pour 50g on top of the frozen sponge.Move the mould to spread out the caramel. Place in the freezer so the caramel sets.

NOTE：You will have leftover soft caramel which can be used in another recipe.


Crémeux caramel

125g caster (superfine) sugar (A)

250g fresh cream 35% fat

25g caster (superfine) sugar (B)

60g egg yolks

38g gelatine mass （1:6）

125g mascarpone

METHOD

Heat the sugar (A) in a saucepan to create a caramel. Heat the cream in the microwave for approximately 90 seconds (it will differ between microwaves so ensure you check it). Add in the hot cream, being careful of the hot steam, and whisk to combine. Remove from the heat and set aside.

Whisk the sugar (B) and egg yolks in a bowl by hand. Pour a small amount of the hot caramel into the yolk and whisk to combine, before transferring the mixture back into the saucepan. Return the saucepan to the heat and cook like an anglaise to between 82˚C and 85˚C, stirring constantly. Be careful not to boil the mixture. Transfer the caramel to a jug and add in the gelatine. Whisk to combine. When it has fully melted, cool down in the fridge to 50˚C, this will take approximately 10-15 minutes.When the caramel has cooled, add the mascarpone and emulsify with a stick blender until smooth.

Transfer to a depositor and place 100g of the crémeux caramel into the insert, on top of the frozen caramel. Return to the freezer to fully set, this will take approximately 2 hours.


Tonka rice pudding

100g fresh cream 35% fat

400g full cream milk

3g tonka bean

25g caster (superfine) sugar

50g arborio rice

50g Callebaut gold couverture 30.4%

METHOD

Combine the milk and cream in a bowl and grate in the tonka bean. Cover and infuse for 24 hours. Strain the mixture into a saucepan and add the sugar. Bring the mixture to a boil, whisking occasionally by hand. When it comes to a boil, add in the pre-rinsed rice and whisk together by hand. Leave for approximately 10-15 minutes for the rice to cook. Pour over the chocolate in a bowl and combine with a spatula. Pour the mixture into a tray with sides so it cools down quicker and cover with plastic wrap touching the surface. Cool in the fridge for a few hours. When the cremeux caramel has set, place 150g of rice pudding on top of the set cremeux caramel. Smooth out with a large angled palette knife. Freeze for a few hours.


Pecan mousse

2u Heilala vanilla bean

180g full cream milk

40g egg yolks

180g Callebaut gold couverture 30.4%

25g gelatine mass

50g pecan paste

301g fresh cream 35% fat

METHOD

Infuse the vanilla beans in the milk overnight. Place the infused milk with the vanilla in a saucepan and add the egg yolks. Cook to between 82˚C and 85˚C while whisking by hand.

Strain the hot mixture over the chocolate in a bowl. Add in the gelatine and whisk by hand to combine. Add the pecan paste and whisk again before emulsifying with a stick blender until smooth. Cool to 34˚C - 35˚C. Semi-whip the cream in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment. Slowly fold the cream into the mousse in 2-3 intervals. Finish by slowly whisking by hand.


Gold oil

100g grapeseed oil

50g metallic gold powder

METHOD

Mix the oil and gold powder together with a stick blender.


Assembly

Unmould the frozen insert and pat down to remove any condensation.

Pour 350g of the pecan mousse into the larger mould. Remove the frozen insert from the fridge and place it on top of the mousse (flat side facing up). Press down gently until it is submerged in the mousse. Place the pecan crunchy disc on top, brushed side down. Place in the freezer for at least 3-4 hours.

Unmold，glaze and decorate with chocolate.
