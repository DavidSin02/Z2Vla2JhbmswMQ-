Hey guys!

I'm very happy to finally present the first installment in my biggest endeavour this year.

In April, the team at Sample and Hold (https://anewdesign.studio/) scanned a lady named, Silvina. My role was to take this photogrammetry data and transform her into a high-quality digi-double for both offline and real-time rendering. This series will focus on the process for the offline renders.

The first part covers how I worked with this data including the modelling and the clean-up process. I hope that this information gives you all a good insight into how I approach scan data as I know it's been something a lot of people have been asking me about.

Look forward to sharing more and see you guys in the next part, which will be about eyes and teeth!


大家好！

4月，Sample and Hold團隊掃描了一位名叫西爾維娜（Silvina）的女士。我的任務是獲取此攝影測量數據並將其轉換為高質量的digi-double，以進行離線和實時渲染。本系列將重點介紹脫機渲染的過程。

第一部分介紹了我如何使用這些數據，包括建模和清理過程。我希望這些信息能使您對我如何處理掃描數據有一個很好的瞭解，因為這是很多人問過我的問題。

software：

第1部分：使用掃描數據


這是我和A New Design Studio的合作項目。


我們給一位名叫西爾維娜（Silvina）的女士進行了攝影測量（photogrammetry）


早在四月，我得到了她的頭部掃描圖，一組高質量albedo與albedo + spec map的參考圖


照片和頭部基礎網格mesh。



The head base mesh looked as follows.


My job was to remodel areas of the face, redirecting the edge flow in some areas as well as reducing the resolution in thelesser-seen areas such as the back of thehead and increasing the resolution around the eyes, nose and mouth.


頭部的基礎網格看起來如下。


我的工作是重塑臉部的區域，將一些區域的佈線重新定向，同時降低後腦勺等較隱蔽的區域的精細度（分辨率resolution），增加眼睛、鼻子和嘴巴周圍的精細度（分辨率resolution）。



The finalised base mesh was wrapped onto the scan in R3DS Wrap making sure to match the features with the topology.


For example, the edge loops that surround the nose and mouth represent the nasolabial fold.


最終確定的基礎網格在R3DS Wrap中被包裹到掃描模型上，確保特徵與拓撲結構相匹配。


例如，圍繞著鼻子和嘴巴的邊緣圈代表了鼻脣部的褶皺。



Once wrapped, both the base and scan were imported into ZBrush. It's not as simple as just projecting the scan onto the model.


You can see from looking at the scan that there are a lot of artefacts around the ears and nostrils, so the process involves masking and projecting onto the model bit-by-bit.


包裹好後，將底座和掃描圖都導入到ZBrush中。這並不像僅僅將掃描圖投影到模型上那麼簡單。


你可以從掃描中看到，耳朵和鼻孔周圍有很多偽影，所以這個過程涉及到蒙版和投影到模型上逐個位的蒙版。



Masks are always blurred to make sure that the projection is smooth and transitions onto the model cleanly.


For parts of the face that the wrap process didn't match exactly, I simply use the move tool to manually reposition areas to align with the scan and then project.


遮罩總是模糊的，以確保投影的平滑和乾淨地過渡到模型上。


對於包覆過程中沒有完全吻合的部分，我只需使用移動工具手動調整區域位置，使其與掃描結果一致，然後進行投影。


頭型流程

-GIF效果演示-


To start,I paint a large mask exposing most of the face and masking out the cavities and the hair.I then project at the lowest subdivision so the model takes the shape of the scan.


Ithen slowly work my way up subdivision levels masking individual areas and projecting.


首先，我畫了一個大的蒙版，把大部分的臉部暴露出來，並把空腔和頭髮遮住。

然後再慢慢地向上分層，把個別區域遮蓋起來，再進行投影。



If I decided to project all at once, not only would the model pick up the unwanted artefacts in the scan, but the cavity areas of themodel such as the eyes, nostrils, mouth and ears would result in large spiky errors that are hard to get rid of.


如果我決定一次投射，不僅模型會把掃描中不需要的偽影撿起來，而且模型的空腔區域，如眼睛、鼻孔、嘴巴、耳朵等都會產生較大的尖狀誤差，難以去除。


The idea is to do the main projection at a low subdivision level. The higher subdivision levels are for including some of the finer detalls from the scan but most of these are small artefacts, so I have to be careful not to project those.


我的想法是在低分層做主投射。較高的細分級別是為了包括掃描中的一些更精細的細節，但是其中大多數都是小的偽像，因此我必須小心不要投射出這些細節。



You can see I' ve decided to pick some subtle details from the neck, eyes and lips but everything else is left smooth at this point.


你可以看到我已經決定從頸部，眼睛和嘴脣中選擇一些細微的細節，但此時其他的東西都保持平滑。

Once I'm happy with the projection,I begin to sculpt areas using the head scan and photographs as reference.You can see below how I sculpted the ears and tweaked the shape of the eye to accommodate for the eyeball and caruncle geometry.


一旦我對投影滿意了，我就開始用頭部掃描和照片作為參考，雕刻一些區域。


你可以在下面看到我是如何雕刻耳朵和調整眼睛的形狀以適應眼球和耳廓的幾何形狀。


上圖：掃描耳朵

下圖：修改後的最終造型


The look of the eye is very important.I always have an eyeball geometry appended in the scene to ensure I am sculpting around the shape to avoid clipping between the two geometries.


眼睛的外觀是非常重要的，我總是在場景中附加一個眼球的幾何圖形，以確保我是圍繞著該形狀進行雕刻，避免兩個幾何圖形之間的剪切。



To work on trickier areas like the corners of the mouth,l used the mask lasso tool in the morph-UV mode to mask and polygroup separate areas. This way it is easier to isolate sections and work on those without distrubing the rest of the sculpt


為了處理一些比較棘手的區域，比如嘴角，我使用了“變形-UV”模式下的“遮罩套索”工具遮罩和多組分開的區域，這樣可以更容易隔離出一些區域，在不影響到其他的雕塑的情況下處理這些區域。


Creating clean eyelids for the eyeballs and caruncle to sit in打造乾淨的眼周，讓眼球和眼角肌都能吻合

To work on trickier areas like the corners of the mouth,l used the mask lasso tool in the morph-UV mode to mask and polygroup separate areas. This way it is easier to isolate sections and work on those without distrubing the rest of the sculpt


為了處理一些比較棘手的區域，比如嘴角，我使用了“變形-UV”模式下的“遮罩套索”工具遮罩和多組分開的區域，這樣可以更容易隔離出一些區域，在不影響到其他的雕塑的情況下處理這些區域。



Like the model, the diffuse texture also needed to be cleaned.This process was done within Mari.


I started by painting out the hair as well as the clothes on her shoulders and proceeded by painting out any artefacts.


I also painted an eyebrow mask and reduced its opacity a litle, which I find allows the XGen hair to blend into the skin better.


和模型一樣，漫反射的紋理也需要清理。這個過程是在Mari內部完成的。


我先把頭髮和她肩膀上的衣服都畫出來，然後再畫出任何手工藝品。


我還畫了一個眉毛 mask，並降低了不透明性，我發現這樣可以讓XGen的頭髮更好地融入皮膚。



The look of the diffuse map was further corrected, which I will demonstrate in a later part


漫反射貼圖的外觀得到了進一步校正，我將在後面的部分中演示


I like to create a procedural layer in Mari to tile red veins like so and reduce the opacity ro around 10-15%.


我喜歡在Mari中創建一個程序層，以平鋪紅色靜脈，並降低10-15%左右的不透明度。

第2部分：牙齒


Teeth and gums base mesh is a unified geometry.I' ll be using maps to distinguish the two materials.


牙齒和牙齦base mesh(基礎網格)是一個整體模型，我將用兩種材質貼圖來區分。




The scan is shown above.


The base mesh was wrapped onto the scan and then projected in ZBrush and cleaned up. The upper and lower UV's are also shown respectively.


掃描如上圖所示。


將base mesh（基礎網格）包裹在掃描得到的模型上，然後在ZBrush裡進行清理。整個牙上部和下部的UV也分別顯示了。



I then carefully painted masks for the top and bottom teeth inside of ZBrush to differentiate between the gums and the teeth.


This mask would later become instrumental in the look-dev process when trying to create convincing materials.


然後，我在ZBrush裡面仔細地給上下牙齒塗上mask蒙版，以區分牙齦和牙齒。


這個mask蒙版在後續的設計過程中將會發揮重要作用。



A general base layer was painted in Mudbox by colour-picking from the reference.


Then Iprojected onto the front 4-6 teeth usingthe cross-polarised photo provided.


Some procedural veins were added to the gums in Mari.This was repeated for the bottom set.


通過從參考中進行選色，在Mudbox中繪製了一個普通的基礎層。


然後我使用提供的交叉極化照片將其投射到前4-6個牙齒上。


在Mari裡，給牙齦添加了一些程序性靜脈紋理，對下組重複進行此操作。



Once the base was finalised,I started adding spherical geometry between the teeth to resemble saliva.


基礎模型確定後，我開始在牙齒之間添加球狀的幾何形狀，使之與唾液相似。


Breakdown of the maps for the teeth.


My thought process when creating these maps was to emulate the look of saliva, which is very apparent in the reference images.


I wanted the specular to not only be broken up by the detall from the scan but also a weight map, which would create these random saliva-like patterns. These effects were done procedurally in Mari.


牙齒貼圖的細分。


在製作這些地圖時，我考慮的是模仿唾液的樣子，在參考圖片中這點非常明顯。


我希望specular 裡不僅要有掃描圖的細節，而且還要有一個權重圖，這樣就會產生這些隨機的類似口水的圖案。這些效果都是在Mari中通過程序化的方式完成的。



When testing, the subsurface is crucial in creating a realistic teeth shader. As you can see from the images above, using the same render settings, the diffusion model creates harsh shadows between each tooth, whereas randomwalk eliminates(most of) the shadowing, which is more accurate.


It also resembles the glassy coating, also known as enamel.


I also tested randomwalk v2, which is very similar but has a little more shadowing than I would have liked.


測試時， subsurface是創建一個逼真的牙齒著色器的關鍵。從上面的圖片中可以看到，使用相同的渲染設置，diffusion model 會在每個牙齒之間創建粗糙的陰影，而randomwalk隨機遊走會消除（大部分）陰影，這更加準確。


它也類似於玻璃塗層，也稱為搪瓷。


我還測試了randomwalk v2，它非常相似，但是陰影比我想要的多。


教程外補充

subsurface：次表面散射（SSS）模擬光線進入對象並在其表面下散射的效果。

常用於大理石、皮膚、樹葉、蠟、牛奶等材質的真實再現


Harsh shadows/醜陋的陰影

A high value for ray depth diffuse to clean any noise and shadowing that occurs between teeth or from the saliva geometries


對深度的漫反射採用較高的射線值可以清除牙齒之間或唾液幾何形狀中產生的一些噪波和陰影。



The tongue was re-used from a previous project and manipulated to match the reference.


I projected the provided cross-polarised imagein Mudbox and used this map to derive all the other maps within photoshop by applying filters and some hand painting.


After creating the tongue's bump map, this was applied inside of ZBrush as displacement and then exported out as a displacement map that would work inside of Arnold.


舌頭是在之前的項目中的，重新使用，並對其進行了操作，以匹配。


我在Mudbox中投射出了提供的交叉極化圖像（ cross-polarised imagein ），然後用這個貼圖在Photoshop中通過應用濾鏡和一些手繪來推導出所有其他的貼圖。


在創建了舌頭的凸點貼圖後，在ZBrush中應用了置換，然後導出為置換貼圖（displacement map），在Arnold中工作。



Siting the teeth inside the base mesh with a smile blendshape applied to test the teeth inside a cavity.


These images are using HDRI lights in the ACES colour space.


將牙齒放置在base mesh內，應用smile blendshape（微笑混合器）的形狀來測試牙洞內的牙齒。


這些圖像都是在ACEScolour space中使用HDRI燈。


教程外補充

什麼是blendshape？

答：單個網格變形以實現許多預定義形狀和任何數量之間組合的技術，在Maya/3ds Max 中我們稱它為變形目標，例如單個網格是默認形狀的基本形狀（例如無表情的面），並且基本形狀的其他形狀用於混合/變形，是不同的表達（笑、皺眉、閉合眼皮），這些被統稱為混合形狀或變形目標。


如何使用 Blend Shape 製作表情呢？

答：要在 Maya 或者3ds Max 裡面製作一個含有 Blend Shape 的形變動畫 Avator。

把Avator導入到 Unity 中給預製的 Avator 添加組件代碼這裡我們叫 BlendShapeDemo.cs，來控制 Avator 的表情變化。代碼如下：


using System.Collections;using System.Collections.Generic;using UnityEngine;public class TestBlendShapeBehaviour : MonoBehaviour { int blendShapeCount; SkinnedMeshRenderer skinnedMeshRenderer; Mesh skinnedMesh; void Awake () { skinnedMeshRenderer = GetComponent<SkinnedMeshRenderer> (); skinnedMesh = GetComponent<SkinnedMeshRenderer> ().sharedMesh; } void Start () { blendShapeCount = skinnedMesh.blendShapeCount; } void OnGUI() { //21，是指模型中設定發生形變張開嘴巴 0:左眼閉合 1:右眼閉合 if (GUI.Button (new Rect (100, 100, 150, 80), "張開嘴巴 && 閉眼")) { skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (0, 100); skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (1, 100); skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (21, 100); } if (GUI.Button (new Rect (100, 200, 150, 80), "閉合嘴巴 && 睜眼")) { skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (0, 0); skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (1, 0); skinnedMeshRenderer.SetBlendShapeWeight (21, 0); } }}

SkinnedMeshRenderer就是含有 blendshapes 列表的渲染器，實施更改其中的權重，來改變其中的表情。
