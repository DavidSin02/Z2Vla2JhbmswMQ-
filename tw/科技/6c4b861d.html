高白空間設計丨AD GIN STUDIO高端影棚

2020-10-29
| 科技

AD GIN STUDIO是GB SPACE（高白空間設計事務所) 設計的一家高端影棚項目，位於北京市朝陽區金盞鎮金盞南路金星祈園K2座。項目總面積360平米，空間由VIP區域和影棚區域兩部分組成，其中VIP區域約160平米，主要包含吧檯區域、會議室及兩個VIP接待室，影棚面積280平米。

AD GIN STUDIO is a high-end photography studio designed by GB SPACE. This project is located at block K2 JinXingQiYuan in Beijing. The total area of the project is 360 square meters. The space consists of two parts： VIP area and studio area. The VIP area is about 160 square meters， including bar area， conference area and two VIP reception rooms. The studio area is 280 square meters.

設計靈感來自於拍攝手法中畫中畫的構圖手法，設計師嘗試在有限空間內創造多層次的視覺效果，通過層疊錯落的的空間記錄人和故事，空間既彼此獨立又有視線上的聯繫，使人遊走在空間中能夠有更多情景感受。八米六的淨高空間為設計師提供了一個良好的條件，設計師在空間中設計了兩個錯層分別安置 了兩個VIP接待室，通過高度區分了公共區域和私密區域兩種功能需求，並由樓梯作為每個空間的溝通路徑，增強了空間的層次感，使立面效果更佳豐富。

The inspiration comes from the photography composition technique of framing within a frame.Designers seek to create multi-level visual effects in a limited space， recording people and stories in the scattered stacking spaces.These independent spaces are visually connected to each other so that people walking in the space will have more situational feelings.The clear height of 8.6 meters allows designers to fully develop their potential.The designers have designed two staggered layers for two VIP reception rooms， distinguishing the functional requirements of public area and private area by different heights.

概念插畫（畫中畫）

空間中的大立面是設計師表達概念的重點打造區域，在聚碳酸酯透明牆板上開窗，利用材質的特性結合燈光設計，使空間呈現出了若隱若現的畫中畫效果。

In addition， by using stairs as the communication path of each space， the layering of the space is enhanced， and the facade effect is significant.The facade in the space is the key area for the designers to express the concept. The window is opened on the translucent polycarbonate wall panels ， and the material is combined with the lighting design to make the space present a looming picture-in-picture effect.

空間選用金屬、水泥漆、水磨石、灰色木為主要材質，牆面上設計了精工細作的造型元素，彷彿截取了畫框一角，延伸畫中畫的設計語言，增加空間的層次感的同時也與粗糙的水泥漆形成顯著的肌理對比。

The material of space is mainly consists of metal， cement paint， terrazzo and gray wood.Designed with exquisite workmanship on the wall， the designers seem to intercept the corner of the picture frame and extend the design language of the picture-in-picture.These design elements reinforce the layering of the space as well as making a significant texture contrast with coarse cement paint.

移動隔斷

基於影棚的特殊使用功能，空間中設計了很多可靈活開閉的門，如入口處即為一個活動大門，開啟時可自然隔離影棚和VIP區域的路徑，使用者可被分流到各個區域中，當有大型拍攝需求時，關閉大門可使各車型車輛自由進出影棚；另外空間中大立面所設計的移動隔門也滿足了不同的使用需求，當門關閉時影棚區域和VIP區域可各自獨立使用，當門開啟移到一側時，影棚區域和VIP區域將融為一體，擴大了影棚拍攝進深，使空間更加開敞靈活。

Based on the special function of photography studio， many flexible doors can be easily opened and closed in the space. For example， the entrance is a movable gate， when it opens， the path of the studio and the VIP area can be naturally separated， and the user can be divided into various areas； when there is a large shooting demand， it allow the various models of vehicles to enter and exit the studio freely by close the gate. In addition， the partition doors designed at the facade in the space also meets different usage requirements. when the door is closed， the studio area and the VIP area can be used independently. When the door is opened and slides to one side， the studio area and the VIP area will be integrated， which will enlarge the depth of the studio and make the space more open and flexible.

VIP接待室的設計秉承了舒適為主兼併功能的原則，在近30平米的空間中，設置了獨立洗手間、mini bar、化妝空間、洗頭臺及用餐休憩空間。設計師精心設計了兩處化妝臺的固定傢俱，現場打造的固定傢俱材質及造型的搭配使整個空間渾然一體，更能凸顯空間的設計語言及獨特性，在軟裝選型細節中也搭配了空間中的金屬材質和灰色木飾面，結合皮質及絲絨傢俱，石材檯面桌子，配合香氛營造氛圍，提高了整個空間的格調與舒適度。

The design of the VIP reception room is based on the principle of comfort and functional. In the space of nearly 30 square meters， there are separate washrooms， minibar， make-up space， shampoo area and dining space.In the two make-up areas， the material and styling of the well-designed furniture integrate into the space， highlighting the design language and the uniqueness of the space.The soft furnishings details are also matched with metal and gray wood finishes in the space， along with leather and velvet furniture， marble table and fragrance， bringing the high style and comfort to the whole space.
