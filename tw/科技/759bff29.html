Some of the members demanded to know why they had been kept in______of the true facts until they reached the present critical stage.

A. secret

B. dark

C. ignorance

D. quest


【答案】C. ignorance (of/about) n. 對……不知；無知，愚昧（如I regret my ignorance on the subject. Ignorance of the law is no excuse. We are in complete ignorance of his plans.）。

in secret 祕密地（注：可以說do sth in secret 祕密做某事，不能說keep sb in secret/in secret of）。

in the dark 在黑暗中：被矇在鼓裡；不讓知道（注：可以說keep/leave sb in the dark about sth 不讓某人知道某事，或keep sth in the dark 對某事保密，不能說keep sb in dark/in dark of sth）。

in quest of 尋找（此處不合邏輯）。

[kwεst] n.探詢;探索;探求物vi.探索;搜尋獵物vt.探尋

[例句]The British establishment seems to be off on another quest for fool's gold.這家英國機構似乎又開始了新一輪異想天開的賺錢大計。
