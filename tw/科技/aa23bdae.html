託福寫作問題大解刨：想舉例子，但沒有好的素材（Q16-21）？

2020-11-14
| 科技

很多同學會疑惑究竟託福舉例子會高分嗎？其實答案是：會的。但是例子一定要合理扣題，如果你說我自己準備了一些萬能的名人例子，那很好，但是前提保證這些例子都能很好的扣住題目。不然分數也會下降。那麼究竟怎麼準備例子素材呢？怎麼讓我們考試的時候用最快的時間把例子寫上去而且獲得高分呢？

下面威學小編給大家整理一下一些通過TPO文章出來的非常棒的例子素材：

Q21:

Do you agree or disagree with following statement：It is easier to be well-educated than it was in the past.

互聯網推動教育的例子

For example, Khan Academy, an innovative online education company, offers high-quality and free-of-charge cramming courses involving mathematics, physics and other high school subjects. A similar type of company called “Coursera” was established with the purposes of making lectures from top-notch universities accessible to the public.

論述國家政策使出國留學變成可能

Compared with developed countries, China is undoubtedly left behind in many aspects including education. In the past, it is unimaginable that one could study abroad because of financial and political reasons. Fortunately, the situation has changed with the implementation of the new policy. More and more Chinese faces begin to appear in western universities.

Q 20:

Do you agree or disagree with following statement：the rules that the society today requires young people to follow and obey are too strict.

談戀愛會不會影響學習的例子

For example, I have two classmates who are in love with each other. They study together, eat together and hanging out together with nobody standing in the way. Even so, they are still among the best in my class.

論證在找工作過程中的潛規則正逐漸淘汰

In my country, getting a decent job without using “backdoor policy” was once considered unimaginable. Whether a young person found a satisfying job or not depended on families’ social backgrounds, rather than real capabilities. Gradually, the public found out that the nepotism easily resulted in corruption, which could badly threaten the principle of social justice and fairness. Today’s young people are not confined to those “unwritten rules” behind the job-hunting.

Q19:

People who develop many different skills are more successful than people who focus on one skill only.

互聯網衝擊下的實體店

For example, having been impacted by online shopping, a large number of owners of physical stores have no choice but to facing a great decline in sales. In contrast, the owners who are good at doing business online are more likely to survive well.

多種技能給人們工作帶來的優勢

Taking my uncle for example, he is a successful salesman and no one is really better than him in sales performance. This is because apart from his skill in selling goods, he also has a good command of English that enables him to deal with overseas customers. Besides, his proficiency in reading people’s minds helps him to take an upper hand in negotiation.

Q18:

During economic crisis, which one of the following expenditures should be reduced by government: library, police or public transportation?

經濟危機時期人們的表現

We can learn from history that financial crisis always comes along with riots and turmoil. Confronted with unemployment, bankruptcy and the decline in living standards, people are likely to swarm into the street to protest against the government.

經濟時期政府的措施

For example, in order to get over the economic crisis as soon as possible, the government must take measures to revitalize the economy by encouraging people to go around in search of new business opportunities, or attracting foreign investments.

Q17:

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement: in modern times, grandparents cannot give their grandchildren useful advice because the world of today are too different than it was in the past.

長輩對晚輩的幫助1

For example, in my high school, I was so-called a straight-A student, but once I screwed up a test by getting a score that kicked me out of top 10 in my class, which disappointed me so much. Hearing about my failure, my grandfather tried to cheer up by his true story about how to smash enemies’ fort after series of defeats when he was in the army. That inspirational story finally put me together.

長輩對晚輩的幫助2

Just imagine s scene that a young girl is dating with handsome-looking guy. However, after a family dinner, the girl’s grandma, by analyzing the man’s way of talking, told her that the man might have an inclination to use family violence. In this case, the advice of grandma surely can impede the girl from stepping into a risky relationship.

長輩的意見雖然過時，但仍有價值

Admittedly, under some circumstances, the advice of grandparents seems to be traditional and conservative. After all, their ways of looking at things are confined to their backgrounds that have somewhat fallen behind the times. However, when it comes to dealing with people or matter, grandparents’ advice really counts because they are the precious summary derived from lessons of successes and failures on their lifetime.

Q16：

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement:children should spend all their time studying and playing and they shouldn’t be required to help with household chores, such as cooking etc.

做家務的好處

For example, if a child wants to study abroad in the future, he or she needs a strong practical ability to survive such as washing clothes and cooking, because there is no one else to rely on.

不做家務的後果

A negative example is my cousin who only cares about himself and becomes aloof to his parents, which is probably caused by his absence in any type of household chores.
