Product Selection Guide of Anhui Hualing Cable Group Co., Ltd.

額定電壓0.6/1kV及以下佈線用銅芯軋紋鋁護套無機礦物絕緣柔性防火電纜

Copper Core Corrugating Aluminum Sheath Inorganic Mineral Insulated Flexible Fireproof Cables with Rated Voltage of 0.6/1kV and Below for Wiring

一、產品用途

I. Product usage

BTLWRZ、BTLWERZ型額定電壓0.6/1kV及以下佈線用銅芯無機礦物絕緣軋紋鋁護套柔性防火電纜相比於普通的阻燃耐火電纜，環保性能更好、阻燃和耐火等級更高，其耐火性能達到950～1000℃，3h，同時通過英國BS6387：2013和BS8491：2008標準規定的線路完成性（單純耐火C、耐火防水W和耐火衝擊Z）試驗和A類阻燃試驗。

Comparing with ordinary flame retardant and fire resistant cables, BTLWRZ and BTLWERZ type cooper core inorganic mineral insulated corrugating aluminum sheath flexible fireproof cables with rated voltage of 0.6/1kV and below for wiring has better environment performance and higher flame retardant and fire resistant rating. Its fire resistance could reach 3h under 3950～1000℃h. Meanwhile, it has passed the circuit integrity (simple fire resistance C, fire resistance and waterproof W and fire resistance impact Z) test and type A fire retardancy test stipulated in BS6387: 2013 and BS8491: 2008 standard.

該電纜廣泛應用於以下場合：

This cable is widely applied to the following occasions:

1、城市重要建築及關鍵領域普通照明、應急照明、火災報警線路供電：高層建築、電視塔、機場候機樓地鐵、輕軌、遂道等。

1. Power supply for general lighting, emergency lighting and fire alarm line of important buildings and key sectors in cities: high-rise buildings, TV towers, metros of departure halls, light rails, tunnels, etc.

2、人員密集的建築物中設備及照明系統供電：購物中心、酒店、醫院、會議中心、體育中心、劇院、圖書館、博物館、數據處理中心等。

2. Power supply for equipment and lighting system of crowded buildings: shopping centers, hotels, hospitals, conference centers, sports centers, theaters, libraries, museums, data processing centers, etc.

3、停放眾多交通工具場合、文物及易爆場合中設備及照明系統供電：停車場、地下車庫、人防、名勝古蹟、石油化工、石油平臺、鋼鐵冶金、電站、核電站、油庫、彈藥庫、工礦等。

Power supply for equipment and lighting system in places parked with lots of vehicles and the places with antiques and are explosive: parking lots, underground garages, civil air defense, scenic spots and historic sites, petrochemical engineering, oil platforms, ferrous metallurgy, power stations, nuclear power stations, oil depots, ammunition depots, industry and mining, etc.

二、主要技術特點

II. Major technical characteristics

本產品僅含少量的有機材料，具有高阻燃(A類)、低煙、無滷和耐火特性，在繼承了BTTZ系列礦物絕緣電纜高度防火特性的同時特殊的結構設計使得電纜耐電壓等級、絕緣電阻和彎曲性能均有大幅提高，運行非常節能，可以連續多芯大截面大長度生產，安裝也回覆到傳統電纜安裝工藝，施工與維護簡便，綜合成本大大降低。其優勢有：

This product only contains small amount of organic material and has high flame retardant, low smoke, halogen free and fire resistant characteristics. In addition to the inheritance of high fireproof characteristic of BTTZ series mineral insulated cables, its special structural design greatly improves voltage resistance grade, insulation resistance and bending property and energy can be saved during operation. Continuous multi-core cables with large section and long length can be produced and the installation also returns to the installation technology of traditional cables. The construction and maintenance is simple and convenient and comprehensive cost is greatly lowered. Its advantages include:

1、既有金屬護套，屏蔽性能好，抗電磁干擾強，電纜的彎曲性能也好。

1. Metal sheath has good shielding property and strong anti-electromagnetic interference. The bending property of cables is also good. 　　

2、接頭少，施工簡單，可靠性高，大大降低投入成本。

2. Less joint, simple construction, high reliability and greatly reduced input cost.

3、多芯最大導體截面可生產到185mm2，克服BTTZ 25mm2以上只能生產單芯的不足。

3. Maximum multi-core conductor section could reach 185mm2, which overcomes the shortage that only single core can be produced for BTTZ above 25mm2.

4、電纜可再生使用，如遇線路改建調整時，電纜可以拆下重複使用。

4. Cables could be reproduced and used. In case line is reconstructed and adjusted, cables could be removed to be repeatedly used.

三、使用特性

III. Use characteristics

1、額定電壓

1. Rated voltage

電纜額定電壓U0/U(Um)為：0.6/1(1.2)kV

Cable rated voltage U0/U(Um) is 0.6/1(1.2)kV

2、採用標準

2. Standards adopted

BS 6387:2013或BS8491：2008在火焰條件下電纜保持電路完整性的性能要求

BS 6387:2013 or BS8491: 2008, the performance requirement for cables to keep circuit integrity under flame condition

GB/T12706.1《額定電壓1kV（Um=1.2KV)到3kV（Um=40.5KV)擠包絕緣電力電纜》

-2016 《額定電壓0.6/1kV及以下銅芯無機礦物絕緣軋紋鋁護套柔性防火電纜》

GB/T12706.1 power cables with extruded insulation for rated voltages from 1 kV(Um=1.2 kV) up to 3 kV(Um=40.5 kV)—2016 copper core inorganic mineral insulation corrugating aluminum sheath flexible fireproof cables for rated voltages of 0.6/1kV and below

3、導體額定工作溫度

3. Rated working temperature of conductor

電纜導體的最高額定工作溫度為90℃。

Maximum rated working temperature of cable conductor is 90℃.

短路時（最長持續時間不超過5s）電纜導體的最高溫度不超過250℃。

During short circuit (maximum lasting time is less than 5s), maximum temperature of cable conductor shall be less than 250℃.

4、敷設環境溫度

4. Laying environment temperature

電纜敷設時的環境溫度應不低於0℃，否則需要事先預熱。

The environment temperature during cable laying shall be higher than 0℃. Otherwise, preheating in advance will be required.

5、成品耐壓試驗

5. Withstand voltage test of finished products

額定電壓0.6/1kV成品電纜耐壓試驗應符合：GB/T12706.1-2008的規定，經受工頻交流3.5kV電壓試驗5min不擊穿。

Withstand voltage test of finished products with rated voltage of 0.6/1kV: according to the stipulation in GB/T12706.1-2008, stand the power frequency AC 3.5kV voltage test for 5min and have no breakdown

6、柔軟性能

6. Flexibility performance

與普通電纜近似，能夠像普通電纜一樣柔軟。

Similar to that of ordinary cables and can be as flexible as that of ordinary cables

7、安全性好

7. Good security

本電纜應能在火焰中正常供電，起動滅火設備，減少火災損失，對人身安全要可靠。較長的製造長度減少了中間接頭隱患。

This cable shall be able to supply power normally in flame, start fire-extinguishing equipment and reduce the loss incurred by fire disaster. It shall be reliable for personal safety. Relatively longer manufacture length reduces the hidden danger brought by intermediate joint.

8、安裝方便

8. Convenient installation

本電纜安裝接頭極少，施工簡單，提高可靠性，降低投入成本。

There are only a few installation joints in this cable, thus the construction is simple, reliability is improved and input cost is reduced.

9、產品附件

9. Product accessories

分支箱、T接端子、穿刺線夾、銅鼻子 ，建議消防線路採用專用終端，當電纜長度達不到線路長度時也可採用中間聯接器聯接，以確保電纜端頭或聯接處也具有防火性能。

Brand box, T wiring terminal, piercing connector and cooper nose. It is suggested fire-fighting lines shall use special terminal. In case the cable length cannot reach the length of the line, intermediate connector could also be used for connection, so as to ensure cable socket or connection part is also fireproof.
