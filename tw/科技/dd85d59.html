中山大學管理學院2018年度前沿方法系列研討班：

增進組織科學研究的穩定性和可信性

2018 ANNUAL SYSBS METHODOLOGICAL WORKSHOP：

Promoting Robust and Reliable Research in Organization Sciences

一、研討班內容簡介

About Robust and Reliable Research Practices

近年來在組織科學和其他多個社會科學領域，都出現了研究結果可重複性的危機。本次研討班將給大家詳細介紹這個危機的來源和克服方法。我們通過實例來講解如何增強研究結論的穩定性和可信性，包括使用事後統計效力分析、元分析，和重複性統計檢驗等方法。我們希望通過此次研討班，推動管理學和組織科學研究者進行更加嚴謹與可信的理論推導、研究設計、統計檢驗和結果報告。

There have been multiple calls in the Organization Sciences for scholars to conduct reproducible, robust, and reliable research, in the wake of a so-called "reproducibility crisis". This workshop introduces to organization researchers in China a thorough understanding of problems associated with HARKing and p-hacking. We will use concrete examples from the management and organizational research literatures to illustrate how to combat with these problems, using approaches including post hoc power analysis, meta-analysis, and statistical reproduction methods. Our goal is to encourage more rigorous and useful research, in terms of theorizing, study design, testing, and reporting.

二、關於研討班及目標學員

About the Workshop and Who Should Attend

中山大學管理學院一向致力於向華人管理學者介紹最前沿的研究方法。本次講座作為中山大學管理學院方法論系列講座第八期，是首次全國範圍內的關於增進組織科學研究的穩定性和可信性的中文講座。本次講座致力於向華人學者介紹如何更好的進行研究設計與統計檢驗，以使研究結果更加穩定可靠。講授者將結合自己的實踐經驗，通過實例進行演示和說明，從而協助學員更穩健地進行組織管理相關研究。

This Workshop is the 8th annual workshop sponsored by SYSBS, featuring applications-oriented workshops focusing on the latest trends in organizational research methods. Led by two experts in the field, as the first national Workshop on Promoting Robust and Reliable Research in Organization Sciences in Chinese, it aims to tutor Chinese scholars about a deep understanding of the methods used for conducting robust and reliable research in management and other disciplines in the Organization Sciences. In addition, the instructors will demonstrate how to apply these methods in their research projects.

溫馨提示（ Reminder） ：

學員應提前熟知與顯著性統計檢驗相關的傳統統計方法（包括t-tests, ANOVA, 線性迴歸, 結構方程模型等）。另外，希望學員對bootstrapping methods和元分析(meta-analysis)也能有一些基礎知識。

Participants should be familiar with statistical methods based on Hypothesis Significance Testing (NHST), including t-tests, ANOVA, linear regressions, and structural modeling. Working knowledge about bootstrapping methods and meta-analysis will be helpful.

四、講授者簡介

Instructor Biographies

汪默，現任佛羅里達大學Warrington商學院Lanzillotti-McKethan傑出學者講席教授，人力資源研究中心主任。他的研究方向包括退休管理及中老年職工就業，外派人員及新員工入職與調適，職業健康心理學，領導力和團隊過程，以及高級計量方法學。他的研究獲得過管理學會（Academy of Management），工業與組織心理學會（Society for Industrial-Organizational Psychology），以及多個學術刊物的學術成就獎。他還獲得了多個學術組織和學會的中期/早期職業成就/貢獻獎，包括管理學會組織行為分會（OB Division, 2017）,美國心理學會（American Psychological Association，2013），行為與腦科學聯合會（Federation of Association in Behavioral and Brain Sciences，2013），工業與組織心理學會（2012）,管理學會人力資源分會（HR Division, 2011）、方法學分會（Research Methods Division, 2011），和職業健康心理學會（Society for Occupational Health Psychology，2009）。他擔任過《牛津退休全書》的主編，目前是學術期刊Work, Aging and Retirement的主編，Journal of Applied Psychology的副主編，及多個其他學術期刊的編委會成員。他也是美國心理學會，心理科學聯合會，和工業與組織心理學會的院士。汪默曾擔任職業健康心理學會主席（2014-2015）以及美國國家科學基金（National Science Foundation）組織科學(Science of Organizations)項目的主任（2014-2016）。

Dr. Mo Wang is the Lanzillotti-McKethan Eminent Scholar Chair at the Warrington College of Business at University of Florida. He is also the Director of Human Resource Research Center at University of Florida. He specializes in research areas of retirement and older worker employment, expatriate and newcomer adjustment, occupational health psychology, leadership and team processes, and advanced quantitative methodologies. He received multiple scholarly achievement awards from Academy of Management, Society for Industrial-Organizational Psychology, and academic journals for his research in these areas. He also received Mid/Early Career Contribution/Achievement Awards from Academy of Management’s OB Division (2017), American Psychological Association (2013), Federation of Associations in Behavioral and Brain Sciences (2013), Society for Industrial-Organizational Psychology (2012), Academy of Management’s HR Division (2011) and Research Methods Division (2011), and Society for Occupational Health Psychology (2009). He was the Editor of The Oxford Handbook of Retirement. He also serves as the Editor-in-Chief for Work, Aging and Retirement, an Associate Editor for Journal of Applied Psychology, and an Editorial Board member for multiple academic journals. He is a Fellow of American Psychological Association, Association of Psychological Science, and Society for Industrial-Organizational Psychology. Dr. Wang was the President of Society for Occupational Health Psychology (2014-2015) and the Director for the Science of Organizations Program at National Science Foundation (2014-2016).

Instructors

張震，現任亞利桑那州立大學W. P. Carey商學院管理系副教授。2008年畢業於美國明尼蘇達大學Carlson商學院，獲得人力資源管理專業博士學位。2000年和2002年於清華大學經濟管理學院獲得會計學本科學位及管理學碩士學位。他的研究方向包括：領導力開發，團隊研究，創業者性格與行為，和高級定量研究方法。2013年他獲得美國管理學會研究方法分會頒發的早期職業成就獎（Early Career Achievement Award）和亞利桑那州立大學頒發的校級傑出研究成果獎（每年全校只有一人獲獎）。同年，他的文章獲得美國工業與組織心理學會（SIOP）頒發的傑出研究獎。他的研究成果發表於多個國際頂級管理學期刊，並擔任這些期刊的編委會成員，包括Academy of Management Journal, Journal of Applied Psychology等等。他目前是學術期刊Personnel Psychology 的副主編。他曾是中國管理研究國際學會（IACMR）和美國管理學會研究方法分會的執行委員會 （Executive Committee）的成員。他的研究理念及活動見諸於國際主要媒體報導，其中包括《Science》、《華爾街日報》、《經濟學人》、《環球郵報》等。

Dr. Zhen Zhang is an associate professor of management and the Dean’s Council of Distinguished Scholar in the W. P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University. Dr. Zhang obtained his Ph.D. in Human Resources and Industrial Relations in 2008 from the University of Minnesota. His research focuses on leadership process and leadership development, work teams and groups, biological basis of work behavior, the intersection between organizational behavior and entrepreneurship, and advanced research methods. His work has appeared in several leading management journals including Academy of Management Journal, Journal of Applied Psychology, Personnel Psychology, Organization Science, Organizational Behavior and Human Decision Processes, and the Leadership Quarterly. Dr. Zhang also published research methods-oriented papers in Psychological Methods and Organizational Research Methods. One of his papers was the winner of the 2013 Wiley Award for Excellence in Survey Research of the Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology. Dr. Zhang is currently an Associate Editor at Personnel Psychology, and he is on the editorial review boards of Academy of Management Journal, Journal of Applied Psychology, Organizational Research Methods, and the Leadership Quarterly.
