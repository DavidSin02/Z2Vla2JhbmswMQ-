簡約時尚 | 預製混凝土構件堆疊而成的多層辦公建築

2020-10-29
| 科技

來源：搜建築（ID：sjz9999）


AMS Boogkeers項目包括修復和改造兩座歷史建築，以及為安特衛普管理學院的主校區建造一座新建築。該項目是市政生態系統的一部分，由初創企業、規模擴大和支持設施組成，其中包括馬路對面的StartUpVillage項目(也是我們辦公室設計的)。


The AMS Boogkeers project comprises the restoration and conversion of two historic buildings and the construction of a new building for the main campus of the Antwerp Management School. The project forms part of a municipal ecosystem of start-ups, scale-ups and support facilities which includes the StartUpVillage project located across the road (also designed by our office).



建築方案主要包括教育空間和相應的支助和行政服務。包括一個地下停車場，自行車儲藏室和檔案室。禮堂和其他高密度空間集中在新建築中，較小的教室和行政程序室位於歷史建築中。


The building programme comprises predominantly educational spaces and corresponding support and administrative services. This includes an underground car park, bicycle storage and archive room. The auditoria and other high-density spaces are centralised in the new-build construction. The smaller classrooms and Executive Program rooms are located in the historic buildings.



新建築以其靈活的佈局和結構為特點，而歷史建築的特定房間序列、循環系統和大廳得到了精心的保護。新建築和歷史建築通過中央門廳和寬敞的多功能空間相連,後者通向一個內部庭院和屋頂花園。


The new building is characterised by its flexible layout and structure, while the historic buildings’ specific room sequence, circulation and saloons have been meticulously preserved. Both the new and historic buildings are connected via a central foyer and generously dimensioned multifunctional space. The latter opens onto an inner courtyard and roof garden.



內庭院是設計中的一個重要空間元素，為Lange Gasthuisstraat紀念碑的後立面提供了重要的周邊空間，同時也為多功能空間和禮堂提供了日光和外部空間和中心感。Mechelse Plein建築的歷史立面按照原設計進行了恢復，而新建築立面的佈局和尺寸是對附近重要歷史公共建築的當代參考。


The inner courtyad represents an important spatial element in the design, providing significant surrounding space for the historic rear facades of the monument in Lange Gasthuisstraat while also furnishing the multifunctional space and auditoria with daylight and a sense of exterior space and centrality. The historic facade of the building on Mechelse Plein is restored according to the original design, while the layout and dimensions of the new-build facade are a contemporary reference to the significant historic public buildings in the immediate vicinity.



新建築的街道側立面是由非常精確的預製混凝土構件堆疊而成，同時為立面提供結構、絕緣和飾面層。面向花園的外牆立面由更精細的混凝土結構組成，與實木門結構形成對比。


The new building’s street-side facades are constructed by the very precise stacking of prefabricated concrete elements, which simultaneously provide structure, insulation and a finishing layer for the facade. The garden-facing facades comprise a more subtle concrete structure contrasted by gate structures in solid wood.
