應對惡劣氣候的創造性建築方案

A Creative Building Solution for the Harsh Climate...

由專築網李韌，劉慶新編譯

Southeast Yukselis學校由伊斯坦布爾的Vertebra建築事務所負責設計，通過獨特的立面設計，很好地應對了烏爾法地區的大陸性氣候，其建築表皮有效地引導了自然通風。

Southeast Yukselis Schools, designed by Istanbul based architectural office Studio Vertebra, offers a good solution to Urfa region's dominant continental climate conditions by it's façade design that creates natural ventilation acting as a second shell.


這座建築位於土耳其烏爾法，建築面積約16000平方米，Southeast Yukselis學校項目包含從幼兒園到高中，再到大學的教育設施，該項目由Vertebra建築事務所設計。

Locating in Urfa province and having an indoor area of 16.000 sqm, Southeast Yukselis Schools project has been designed to facilitate education up to university, from kindergarten to high school by Studio Vertebra.


建築師通過體塊的設計讓不同教育程度的孩子們都有獨立的內部空間、出入口，以及院落。建築師在設計時主要考慮了各個體塊的均衡與穩定，不同體塊之間通過“L”型空間相連接。

Block decisions have been made to provide separate interior circulation areas, entrances and courtyards for different education levels (high school, middle school, primary school, kindergarten). Integration of the blocks and balance have been considered while interpreting the multi-input requirement program. Blocks with different functions have been constructed by two blocks in "L" shape.


在設計建築立面時，建築師充分考慮了區域氣候特徵、地勢狀況等因素。

Regional climate data, terrace usage, and final impact factors have been considered while designing the façade of the construction.


該地區的大陸性氣候特徵較強，並且常年氣溫較高，因此，建築表皮的設置有助於室內空氣的流通。另外，表皮也有效地隔絕了部分來自太陽的熱量，例如建築的南面、東南面，以及西面都包裹著表皮。而在表皮內的牆體上建築師則設計了多個開窗，從而讓立面形態更加獨特。

The project is located in a region where the continental climate is dominant and has high temperatures. For this reason, the façade has been designed as a second shell to contribute to air conditioning. The placed shell functions as an insulation layer. First, the facades that are exposed to direct sunlight, eg. south, east-south-south, and west, have been covered. The plain approach preferred at the remaining façade surfaces emphasized the shell by creating the impression of window-wall.


建築表皮主要用於遮陽。這種連續的表皮形式為建築帶來一定的識別性，同時也讓室內空間成為建築的焦點。

The shell of the building continues in the areas with terraces and functions as a shading element. This continuity creates an identity to the shell and encourages the transformation of terraces into living spaces.


為了讓建築更加自然，建築師為表皮系統選用了大地色材質。而建築的原有牆體則呈現白色，這樣便能夠讓表皮以一種毫不誇張的方式脫穎而出。

Much more natural impression has been ensured by preferring earth tones on the façade which has a strong impact with its form. By preferring white for the remaining surfaces, the effect of shell has been emphasized without exaggeration.



室內裝飾的靈感則來源於調色板，設計師的思路是儘可能地表達空間效果。門廳的樓梯不僅僅只是一個垂直交通樞紐，它還是學生各項活動的溝通場所。在該項目中，設計師重複運用了一些設計策略，例如自助餐廳30m長的餐桌，亦或是門廳內50m長的儲物牆體，這樣的設計方式讓整個建築空間變得靈活連貫，從而更具吸引力。

Interior decoration approach has been inspired by corporate color palette and the idea was to transform objects into spaces. The monolithic staircase on the lobby floor is not just a vertical circulation element, but also it supports many planned and unplanned social activities of the students. This approach is repeated throughout the project, such as 30m-long table in the cafeteria or a 50m2 wall reserved for students in the foyer area.







項目信息：

項目名稱：Southeast Yukselis學校

項目類型：教育建築

建築設計/室內設計：Vertebra建築事務所

地點：土耳其，尚勒烏爾法

總面積：16000平方米

項目年份：2017年
